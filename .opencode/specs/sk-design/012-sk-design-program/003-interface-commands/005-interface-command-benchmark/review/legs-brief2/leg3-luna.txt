## Creation/Remediation Artifact - Interface Direction Spec

### Direction: “Ship at Your Pace”

A confident developer-tool pricing section built around a calm editorial hierarchy, compact monospace metadata, and one deliberately dark Pro plan. The visual signature is a “release rail”: a thin cobalt rule and small numbered markers that connect the three plans like stages in a delivery pipeline.

### Layout

- Full-width section on `#F7F8FA`.
- Eyebrow in mono: `PLANS / 01`.
- Heading: “Ship at your pace.”
- Supporting line: “Start with a focused workflow. Add collaboration when the release cadence demands it.”
- Three equal-width cards in a 12-column grid:
  - Free: columns 1–4
  - Pro: columns 5–8
  - Team: columns 9–12
- On mobile, stack cards with Pro first in visual order.
- A cobalt release rail runs behind the card headers and terminates at a small `→` marker.
- Pro card is raised `8px`, uses `#0E1B2A`, and carries a cobalt top rule plus `RECOMMENDED` label.

### Plan content

#### Free — “For proving the loop”

`$0 / forever`

- 2 private projects
- 10,000 build minutes per month
- Preview deployments for every pull request
- Community support

CTA: `Create free workspace`

#### Pro — “For shipping every week”

`$24 / seat / month`

- Unlimited private projects
- 100,000 build minutes per month
- Preview environments with instant rollback

CTA: `Start Pro trial`

#### Team — “For scaling the release”

`$49 / seat / month`

- 500,000 build minutes per month
- SSO, role-based access, and audit logs

- Priority support with migration guidance

CTA: `Talk to sales`

### Featured-tier treatment

- Pro receives the strongest contrast and visual weight.
- `RECOMMENDED` appears as a compact cobalt-on-white pill above the plan name.
- Price and CTA use the highest luminance contrast.
- The Pro card’s border changes from `#2E5BFF` to `#83A2FF` on hover.
- No glow, animated gradient, or excessive shadow.
