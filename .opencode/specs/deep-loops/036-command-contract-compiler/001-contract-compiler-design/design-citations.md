## Setup Authority Verdict

The seed captures the three setup authority categories but not their full line ranges. The command router owns mode parsing and the YAML start condition (`review.md:90-97`), the presentation asset owns prompt behavior and setup/default tables (`deep_review_presentation.txt:1-3`, `deep_review_presentation.txt:60-88`, `deep_review_presentation.txt:90-135`), and the shared auto-mode contract owns the three-tier auto rules (`auto_mode_contract.md:25-56`). Therefore the 3-way setup authority is structurally captured, but the seed should expand the presentation citation beyond `deep_review_presentation.txt:1-23` and `deep_review_presentation.txt:13-23`.
