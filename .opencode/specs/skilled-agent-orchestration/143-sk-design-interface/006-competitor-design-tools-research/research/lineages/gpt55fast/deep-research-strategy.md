## 7. WHAT WORKED

- Primary docs first: official docs for v0, Lovable, Figma Make, and Subframe provided enough feature detail to avoid marketing-only claims.
- Deduping against packet 005 early kept the recommendation focused on net-new patterns rather than repeating the Claude Design fidelity loop.
- Separating design judgment (`sk-design-interface`) from canvas/CLI mechanics (`mcp-magicpath`) made ADOPT / ADAPT / SKIP decisions crisp.
- Treating SKIP as a first-class outcome preserved the anti-default and lean-CLI boundaries.

---

## 8. WHAT FAILED

- A Lovable `visual-edits` URL returned 404; the current docs place that capability under `preview-toolbar.md`.
- An initial Figma Make article URL resolved to a broader Figma UI3 page; the category and specific article pages supplied the correct Make evidence.
- Some adjacent-product docs were too broad or marketing-heavy; they were used only as weak corroboration, not as core recommendations.
- No completed sibling `opus48-claude2` iteration outputs were present when this lineage synthesized, so cross-lineage reconciliation is left to the merge host.

---

## 9. EXHAUSTED APPROACHES (do not retry)

### Hosted product cloning - BLOCKED (iterations 1-5)
- What was tried: considered v0 deployment, Lovable Cloud, Figma Make publishing, Framer site publishing, and Anima app hosting.
- Why blocked: platform hosting and account/workspace governance are outside the two local CLI skills.
- Do NOT retry: do not recommend hosted deployment, billing, admin, or public publishing features for this packet.

### Preset chooser adoption - BLOCKED (iterations 2 and 5)
- What was tried: evaluated Lovable design questions and curated palettes/layouts as potential local skill features.
- Why blocked: copying them as presets conflicts with `sk-design-interface` anti-default rules.
- Do NOT retry: do not turn design inventory data into a chooser.

### Automated subtle visual diff - BLOCKED (iterations 2 and 5)
- What was tried: evaluated browser testing as a visual quality gate.
- Why blocked: Lovable documents browser testing as unreliable for subtle design details.
- Do NOT retry: use screenshot-supported design judgment, not blind pixel-diff automation, as the fidelity gate.

## 10. RULED OUT DIRECTIONS

- v0-style full-stack deployment/backend agent for these skills: platform scope, not local CLI-skill scope.
- Lovable-style preset palette/font/layout chooser in `sk-design-interface`: conflicts with anti-default mandate.
- Figma Make-style local Git branch/PR management in `mcp-magicpath`: belongs to sk-git/workflow tooling.
- Subframe-style two-way source-of-truth for generated component code: use generated-file plus wrapper boundaries instead.
- Hosted live multiplayer canvas, public publishing, billing, enterprise admin, or backend setup: out of scope.
