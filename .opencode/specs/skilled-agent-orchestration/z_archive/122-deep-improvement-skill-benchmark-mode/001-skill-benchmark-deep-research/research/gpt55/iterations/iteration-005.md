This prevents "zero dangling refs" from becoming an impossible criterion because `descriptions.json` and archive specs legitimately record old renames. 2. Use a two-pass compatibility strategy in advisor: canonical id becomes `deep-improvement`, but old prompts such as `deep-agent-improvement`, `sk-deep-agent-improvement`, and `/deep:start-agent-improvement-loop` should remain aliases that resolve to the new id.
