## Findings

The safe dependency order is: **(A) restore readable benchmark topology; (B) generate and commit the six-mode leaf manifest; (C) independently author typed gold and validate its joins; (D) establish a same-mode, same-corpus typed baseline; (E) change router maps only for pair-level failures exposed by D**.
