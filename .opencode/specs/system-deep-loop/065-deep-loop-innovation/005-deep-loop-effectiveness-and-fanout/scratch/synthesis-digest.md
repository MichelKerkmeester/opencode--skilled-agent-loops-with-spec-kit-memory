## D. Insights by primary subsystem


### deep-ai-council (22)
- Heterogeneous coordination and effective independence are compatible only as phases: isolated generation first, normalized cross-review second, and separately scored selection last. _(ev: https://github.com/massgen/MassGen and https://github.com/microsoft/conductor, high)_
- Model agreement should be capped by effective-independence group and used only as a score bonus; held-out task-stratified calibration should govern model reliability, with uncalibrated models marked unknown rather than assigned a guessed weight. _(ev: https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/AAAI/article/view/5860 and https://github.com/smartyfh/LLM-Uncertainty-Bench, high)_
- Effective independence must be estimated from correctness-conditioned error vectors: retain per-task errors and compute pairwise Q, phi correlation, disagreement, and double-fault; model/provider diversity is only a prior. _(ev: https://github.com/scikit-learn-contrib/DESlib/blob/master/deslib/util/diversity.py and https://research.bangor.ac.uk/en/publications/measures-of-diversity-in-classifier-ensembles-and-their-relations/, high)_
- A correlation-adjusted weighted vote count can be reported as N_eff=(sum(w))^2/(w^T R w); it distinguishes three nominal seats from three independent pieces of evidence and naturally incorporates unequal judge competence. _(ev: https://mc-stan.org/docs/2_31/reference-manual/effective-sample-size.html and https://journals.ametsoc.org/view/journals/clim/24/9/2010jcli3814.1.xml, med)_
- Seat admission should require positive held-out marginal gain in log loss or Brier score, with a bootstrap interval, because low correlation without competence is noise rather than useful independence. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/1907.07384 and https://github.com/scikit-learn-contrib/DESlib, med)_
- Generator, detector, scorer, orchestrator, and target must have separate capabilities and blinded information surfaces; self-recognition evidence shows that assigning different role names inside one shared context is not sufficient. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.13076 and https://github.com/ArjunPanickssery/self_recognition, high)_
- Panel composition and aggregation are separate problems: cross-family panels reduce ordinary bias, while robust aggregation such as a geometric median protects against a biased or collapsed minority that averaging cannot tolerate. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.18796 and https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30931, high)_
- Anytime-valid LLM stopping guarantees exist for repeated terminal samples: MMC certifies the modal answer and CGES stops on posterior concentration, but neither handles a state-changing agent trajectory. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.17472 ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02603, high)_
- Split each expensive branch into plan selection and execution, with separate reward distributions and budgets; execute only plans whose expected utility or upper confidence bound remains competitive. _(ev: Adaptive Test-Time Reasoning via Reward-Guided Dual-Phase Search — https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.25420, high)_
- Replace count-based council quorum with a two-stage gate: calibrated single-leaf acceptance followed by confidence-weighted evidence accumulation, discounted by shared model, prompt, evidence, and ancestry. _(ev: Reliability-Aware Adaptive Self-Consistency — https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02970, high)_
- Bandwagon mitigation requires information isolation, not a stronger anti-sycophancy prompt: blind author identity, model identity, seat scores, and vote counts until all independent judgments are committed. _(ev: https://github.com/Joyyang158/Reasoning-Bias-Detector, high)_
- A swapped-order pair should be the atomic adjudication unit; disagreement between the two orders is an abstention or escalation signal, not a tie to break arbitrarily. _(ev: https://arena.ai/blog/arena-hard/, high)_
- Bayesian reliability and bias coefficients cannot be learned reliably from a single 2-3-seat council; they need persistent calibration profiles versioned by model, judge prompt, rubric, and schema fingerprint. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02104 and https://github.com/lmarena/PPE, med)_
- Debate is most justified when generators have evidence or reasoning capacity unavailable to the judge; route to bounded debate on high expected value of information, then let a separately instantiated judge decide. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.06782 and https://github.com/ucl-dark/llm_debate, high)_
- Constitutional adjudication should be a versioned hard-constraint layer with principle-specific findings and lexicographic precedence, authored independently from the judge; it should not substitute for factual or execution evidence. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.07814, med)_
- Nominal council size is misleading: record dependence-adjusted effective seat count from held-out residual correlations and use it in the convergence gate. _(ev: https://machinelearning.apple.com/research/correlated-llm-evaluation-panels, high)_
- Inter-judge agreement must not count as alignment unless effective-rank and human-oracle-subspace diagnostics also pass; correlated agreement can encode shared bias. _(ev: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/publication/the-geometry-of-llm-as-judge-why-inter-llm-consensus-is-not-human-alignment/, high)_
- Council dispatch should be selective: calibrated risk-coverage curves can decide when a cheap seat is sufficient and when escalation buys enough expected agreement to justify its cost. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.18370, high)_
- When oracle labels are absent, judge-specific discrimination inferred from pairwise probabilities and cycle consistency is a better fallback than uniform weighting, but remains an unsupervised proxy rather than human calibration. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16610, high)_
- Council degeneration is detectable from stance trajectories: classify every flip as toward evidence, the previous majority, the user, or a specific peer, then derive pairwise influence concentration and effective independent-seat count. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02668, med)_
- Council adjudication is naturally a cost-sensitive contextual-bandit problem whose action value is expected downstream-loss reduction minus execution and latency cost, not disagreement or entropy reduction alone. _(ev: https://vowpalwabbit.org/docs/vowpal_wabbit/python/latest/tutorials/off_policy_evaluation.html, high)_
- Adaptive seat routing makes naive before-versus-after evaluation invalid; every seat_selected event needs its available-action set, selection propensity, router version, and eventual outcome, with deployment blocked when overlap diagnostics fail. _(ev: https://vowpalwabbit.org/docs/vowpal_wabbit/python/latest/tutorials/off_policy_evaluation.html, high)_

### runtime/state-jsonl-checkpointing (9)
- A resolved invocation fingerprint should be a pre-spawn JSONL receipt so retries and resumes can prove they reused the same model, effort, tool policy, and sandbox contract. _(ev: Transfer from the structured spawn contracts in openai/codex, google-gemini/gemini-cli, and anomalyco/opencode, high)_

- A leaf receipt should be a content-addressed commit binding logical identity, manifest revision, inputs, prompt, executor/model/tool capabilities, artifact schema, and artifact digest. Reuse requires both fingerprint compatibility and artifact verification. _(ev: https://github.com/nextflow-io/nextflow/blob/master/docs/reference/process.md; https://snakemake.readthedocs.io/en/stable/executing/cli.html; https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20989, high)_
- A durable receipt must distinguish effect completion from workflow completion: completed journal entries are reusable, but an effect that began before its result was committed remains potentially duplicated and must project to unknown. _(ev: https://docs.restate.dev/develop/ts/durable-steps ; https://www.restate.dev/blog/why-we-built-restate ; https://docs.dbos.dev/python/tutorials/workflow-tutorial, high)_
- Replay compatibility is an event-level contract, not a single runtime version. Restate blocks journal mismatches, Temporal routes or patches in-flight histories, and LangGraph warns that reordered positional task or interrupt calls can bind old results to the wrong operation. _(ev: https://docs.restate.dev/services/versioning ; https://github.com/temporalio/temporal/blob/main/docs/worker-versioning.md ; https://docs.langchain.com/oss/python/langgraph/backward-compatibility, high)_
- Statistical evidence must be scoped to a versioned state-policy-evaluator fingerprint; after evidence, prompt, tool, or evaluator drift, prior arm samples are not automatically exchangeable and the certificate must reset or use an explicitly justified Markovian model. _(ev: https://papers.neurips.cc/paper_files/paper/2019/hash/71887f62f073a78511cbac56f8cab53f-Abstract.html, high)_
- Temporal-style replay survives LLM nondeterminism when the LLM output is treated as an immutable history observation; replay determinism applies to the orchestration consuming that observation, not to regenerating it. _(ev: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/durable-task/common/durable-task-orchestrations, high)_

- A sequence-indexed cached-call log is sufficient only while host-call order and invocation identity remain compatible; safe reuse across prompt, model, tool-schema, or code changes needs a versioned replay fingerprint and logical branch-step key. _(ev: https://github.com/ThousandBirdsInc/chidori, med)_

### runtime/fan-out-fan-in (9)

- Deterministic fan-in has two layers: freeze a deterministic admission snapshot of committed leaf receipts, then fold that snapshot in logical-leaf-ID order. Stable sorting alone covers only the second layer. _(ev: nextflow-io/nextflow docs/reference/process.md fair and cache contracts; local runtime/scripts/fanout-merge.cjs, high)_
- Logical effect identity and attempt identity must be separate. The logical ID and downstream idempotency key remain stable across retries, handoffs, salvage, and process restarts; attempt IDs change and preserve forensic history. _(ev: temporalio/sdk-go Activity identity model; https://docs.dbos.dev/python/tutorials/workflow-tutorial ; https://docs.restate.dev/references/architecture, high)_
- Checkpoint recovery should preserve branch-local successes. - Conditional fan-in should be staged: evaluate a cheap seed wave, skip later waves on qualified agreement, and reserve the next wave's budget only when a persisted WIDEN transition fires. _(ev: https://github.com/volcano-sh/volcano/blob/master/docs/design/Network%20Topology%20Aware%20Scheduling.md, high)_

- Progressive fan-in needs immutable provisional projections and a separate final ACCEPT barrier; cancellations and late completions must be recorded without altering the frozen projection. _(ev: https://docs.aws.amazon.com/step-functions/latest/dg/state-map-distributed.html, high)_
- A budget controller can consume reward-distribution shape, reward sparsity, remaining budget, and branch history, then emit sampling width, depth, verifier effort, or stop as explicit actions. _(ev: Reward Model Generalization for Compute-Aware Test-Time Reasoning — https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.18065, high)_

### runtime/dedup-novelty (8)
- Split registry semantics into monotone observation events and non-monotone novelty adjudication. Findings, evidence, hashes, and similarity edges can merge without coordination; canonical representatives and final novelty credit require ordered fan-in or a coordinated commit. _(ev: Keeping CALM: https://arxiv.org/abs/1901.01930, high)_
- Make semantic clustering independent of process arrival order: sort immutable events by logical branch and sequence, use approximate search only for candidate generation, recompute exact similarity edges, then fold the fixed edge set with deterministic union-find and a stable representative tie-breaker. _(ev: Autobase deterministic apply/replay contract: https://github.com/holepunchto/autobase, high)_
- Use a two-speed duplicate path: exact normalized hashes can be reserved atomically with CAS, while semantic similarity remains an append-only claim followed by reducer adjudication. This avoids serializing every embedding comparison through a global lock. _(ev: etcd transaction and watch APIs: https://etcd.io/docs/v3.6/learning/api/, high)_
- Semantic dedup must be a reversible projection: immutable raw findings remain in JSONL, while equivalence, contradiction, cluster membership, and rank are attached as versioned judgments. _(ev: https://github.com/MinishLab/semhash and local runtime/scripts/fanout-merge.cjs, high)_
- Use a two-layer graph: Leiden clusters unsigned topical affinity, while CONTRADICTS, SUPERSEDES and validity events form a separate epistemic overlay. Feeding contradiction edges into ordinary Leiden as positive weights would cluster opposition as agreement. _(ev: https://github.com/graspologic-org/graspologic/blob/main/graspologic/partition/leiden.py ; https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.08473, high)_
- Community IDs require a lifecycle independent of Leiden's integer labels: match adjacent snapshots by maximum-weight Jaccard overlap and append born, retained, split, merged and retired events with a clustering-input fingerprint. _(ev: https://github.com/graspologic-org/graspologic/blob/main/graspologic/partition/leiden.py ; https://github.com/microsoft/graphrag/blob/main/graphrag/index/operations/cluster_graph.py, high)_
- Separate settledNovelty from divergencePressure. Coverage and resolved uncertainty advance the former; under-covered communities, boundary uncertainty and unresolved critical contradictions drive the latter. - Append assertions before resolving them, but make resolution an explicit bitemporal event. Exact-hash or embedding dedup is evidence consolidation, not truth maintenance, and cannot safely decide whether two scoped claims coexist or one supersedes another. _(ev: https://github.com/mem0ai/mem0/blob/main/mem0/memory/main.py ; https://github.com/getzep/graphiti/blob/main/graphiti_core/edges.py, high)_

### runtime/convergence (7)
- A robust stuck detector needs two fingerprints: a canonical transition fingerprint for period-1/2/3 repetition and a progress-state fingerprint for changed artifacts, evidence, execution results, or environment state. Repetition without state stagnation is not a hard stop. _(ev: https://github.com/OpenHands/software-agent-sdk/blob/main/openhands-sdk/openhands/sdk/conversation/stuck_detector.py ; https://github.com/browser-use/browser-use/blob/main/browser_use/agent/views.py, high)_
- Termination should be conjunctive for correctness but staged for cost: cheap novelty, cycle, coverage, and execution checks first; invoke a critic only for a plausible stop candidate, then require quality and execution evidence before declaring convergence. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27009, med)_
- Path-covering termination is a graph property: each reachable SCC containing a retry, handoff, callback, tool invocation, model call, or state-growing operation must consume a monotone root lease and expose a terminal exit. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01641, high)_
- A concrete rule is to model STOP as an arm and stop when every continuation arm's anytime upper confidence bound, net of typed cost and risk, is below the lower confidence bound of stopping plus epsilon. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/1602.04589, high)_
- Convergence must be a two-key decision: internal exhaustion or agreement nominates STOP, while an independent held-out or invariant witness authorizes it. _(ev: https://github.com/ml-research/llms-gaming-verifiers; https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15149, high)_
- Loop failure should be classified by causal mechanism—epistemic collapse, proxy capture, conformity cascade, objective drift, or operational degeneration—not merely by terminal symptoms such as low novelty or high cost. _(ev: https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/content/microsoftcorp/microsoft/bade/documents/products-and-services/en-us/security/Taxonomy-of-Failure-Modes-in-Agentic-AI-Systems-v2-0.pdf, high)_

### runtime/budget-cost (5)
- Absolute deadline propagation plus separate attempt throttling prevents child calls from laundering elapsed time or failure history; JSONL should reconstruct both the root lease and per-transition attempt receipts. _(ev: https://grpc.io/docs/guides/deadlines/ ; https://grpc.io/docs/guides/retry/, high)_

- Ancestor budgets should compose conjunctively rather than through precedence: a leaf call must fit its attempt, lineage, mode, and session limits simultaneously; one exceeded hard limit rejects the lease. _(ev: https://github.com/envoyproxy/ratelimit and https://docs.litellm.ai/docs/proxy/users, high)_
