Sourcing & Relationships

We buy direct,
not through a spreadsheet.

We source from producers in Ethiopia’s Yirgacheffe highlands, the volcanic slopes of Guatemala’s Huehuetenango, and family-run fincas in Colombia’s Huila department. Each relationship is built on annual visits, shared cupping sessions at origin, and pricing that averages 2.4× the Fair Trade floor.

We green-buy in lots of 300 to 1,200 pounds — small enough to trace a coffee back to a single washing station or a named producer group, large enough to build multi-year commitments. Our sourcing calendar runs on the harvest, not on quarterly procurement targets.
