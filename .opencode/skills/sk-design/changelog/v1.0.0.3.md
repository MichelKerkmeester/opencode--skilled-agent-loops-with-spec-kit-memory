# v1.0.0.3 - impeccable build/visual adoption

**Released:** 2026-06-27

This release adopts the build-facing craft from a third external corpus — the `impeccable` design skill — into the design family. impeccable is the most directly comparable corpus studied so far (a consolidated design skill with a register, an audit and anti-slop posture, and opinionated numeric design laws), so most of its structure was already encoded after the earlier two adoptions. A cross-model completeness sweep corrected one under-claim: a set of specific numeric typography, color, and layout rules that earlier passes assumed were already present turned out to be absent. This release lands them, plus a handful of structural refinements, across four scope-locked build phases. No new mode; no second register, score, detector engine, prose validator, or live-mode workflow. Every edit was applied by a quality-tier executor and then independently re-verified by a fresh reviewer with no build context. All edits are additive guidance; nothing changes runtime behavior.

> Source research: the impeccable study packet (12 converged iterations + a cross-model sweep) under `154-sk-design-parent`.
> Build phases: foundations, interface, audit, and motion + md-generator (Level 3 each), each independently verified.

---

## Foundations

The largest tier — specific numeric typography, color, and layout craft.

#### Typography
`typography_system.md` gained web-font loading performance (`font-display: swap`/`optional`, metric-matched fallbacks, preload of the critical weight, a variable-vs-static threshold), a fluid `clamp()` bound (max no more than about 2.5x the min), OpenType polish (diagonal fractions, real small caps, ligature control, explicit kerning), an all-caps tracking range (5-12% for short uppercase labels), and a three-axis light-on-dark type compensation (line-height, letter-spacing, and weight).

#### Color and layout
`palette_theming.md` gained a physical-scene grounding step (write one sentence of who/where/ambient-light/mood before choosing light vs dark) and dangerous color-combination warnings (red/green, blue/red, yellow/white) with a note that roughly 8% of men have a color-vision deficiency. `layout_responsive.md` gained the breakpoint-free `repeat(auto-fit, minmax(280px, 1fr))` tile-grid recipe.

## Interface

`real_ui_loop.md` gained a guarded native-image visual-direction branch: when work is net-new, ambiguous, or image-led at mid-fidelity or higher and native image generation is available, confirm a palette, produce one to three mock directions, and get approval before writing code. It is a guarded refinement of the existing optional gate, not a new mandatory step. `ux_quality_reference.md` gained a first-value onboarding floor (skip/dismissal always available, contextual just-in-time teaching), a full eight-state interactive table, and the `:focus-visible` ring technique (outline-offset, 3:1 ring contrast). `copy_and_mock_data.md` gained selected UI-copy tells as auditable examples (hedge stacks, competitor-swappable copy, structural prose tells) and a translation-expansion table — examples, not an enforced prose validator.

## Audit

`anti_patterns_production.md` gained a design-source drift finding (a committed design-token or `DESIGN.md` export names the source of truth, but the rendered surface uses hard-coded or off-token values) and a semantic z-index scale (dropdown, sticky, modal-backdrop, modal, toast, tooltip) that flags magic numbers like 999/9999. `hardening_edge_cases.md` gained an overlay/top-layer clipping case (an absolute dropdown trapped inside an overflow container; the fix escapes to the top layer via the native dialog/popover API, fixed positioning, or a portal).

## Motion and md-generator

`advanced_craft.md` gained a high-ambition effects guardrail: propose the approach before building an ambitious effect (name the effect, the materials, the performance budget, and the reduced-motion fallback). `quality_checklist.md` gained a primitive-vs-semantic token-naming check (primitive tokens carry raw values; semantic tokens carry a role, and a role name must mean something).

## Ruled out

impeccable's structural systems were deliberately not imported: no new mode, no second register (the shared register already encodes the brand-vs-product posture and the color-strategy axis), no second scoring system, no anti-pattern detector engine, no wholesale prose validator, no live-mode browser workflow, and no document token-seed mode. The per-model defect catalog and the color-strategy axis were already covered. A cross-model adversarial review confirmed every one of these rulings.
