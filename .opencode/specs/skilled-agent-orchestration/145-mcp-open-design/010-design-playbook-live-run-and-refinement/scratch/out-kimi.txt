### TASK ID-001

**Subject grounding**
- **Subject:** An artisan letterpress studio (custom invites, posters, business cards, fine-edition prints).
- **Audience:** Boutique brands, wedding clients, designers, and print enthusiasts who value tactility and craft.
- **Page's one job:** Convince visitors that this studio can make their project feel physically premium and unmistakably theirs.

**Token system plan**

| Token | Role | Value |
|---|---|---|
| `newsprint` | Primary background | `#F2F0E9` — cool, uncoated-stock white, not warm cream |
| `lampblack` | Primary text / ink | `#1A1A1A` — dense, slightly warm black |
| `linen` | Secondary background / texture tone | `#E8E4D9` — raw cotton paper |
| `sienna-impression` | Warm accent | `#8B4D3A` — ink-stained sienna, deeper and browner than terracotta |
| `metal-type` | Muted utility | `#6B6B6B` — lead type grey |
| `wax-seal` | Signature risk accent | `#B83A3A` — a single sealing-wax red, used once |

**Typography roles**
- **Display:** A condensed wood-type-inspired face (e.g., *League Gothic* or *Bebas Neue*) for headlines—letterpress posters historically use bold condensed wood type, not another high-contrast editorial serif.
- **Body:** *Source Serif 4* or *Crimson Pro* for long reading.
- **Utility:** *IBM Plex Mono* for small caps, weights, paper specs, and pricing.

**Layout concept**
Asymmetric editorial layout: a large left column with a "pressed" headline, generous margins, and a narrow right sidebar listing paper stock, ink color, and impression depth. One strong horizontal rule separates hero from process; no hairline grids.

**Signature element**
A hero headline with a CSS deboss/inked impression effect: layered `text-shadow` plus a subtle `box-shadow` inset so the type looks physically pressed into the paper background.

**Anti-default critique**
- **Moving off "cream + high-contrast serif + terracotta":** This is the dominant AI-default artisan look. Instead of `#F4F1EA` + Playfair + terracotta, I chose a cooler `newsprint` base and a condensed wood-type display, because letterpress identity comes from physical type, not editorial fashion.
- **Moving off "near-black + acid accent":** That reads tech/agency. The studio is tactile, so the dark value is `lampblack` (ink) and the single bold accent is `wax-seal` red, not neon.
- **Moving off "broadsheet hairlines + zero radius":** Newspaper grids feel mass-produced. I replaced hairline columns with one strong rule and generous whitespace, letting the impression effect carry the personality.

### TASK ID-003

Quality-floor grading for `.opencode/specs/skilled-agent-orchestration/154-design-skill-integration-test/designs/mimo/01-meridian-roasters.html`.

**Failing rules and fixes**

1. **Color contrast — footer links on dark background**  
   - **Rule:** WCAG AA normal text needs 4.5:1.  
   - **Fail:** `--slate` `#5A6B7A` on `--ink-water` `#1B2A3D` = **2.64:1**. Used for `.footer__links a`, `.footer__tagline`, and `.footer__bottom`.  
   - **Fix:** Use `--fog-warm` `#D0D8E0` or a lighter `#8A9BAA` for footer text.

2. **Color contrast — copper eyebrows on dark and light backgrounds**  
   - **Rule:** WCAG AA normal text needs 4.5:1.  
   - **Fail:** `--copper-oxide` `#B86F4A` on `--ink-water` = **3.74:1**; on `--fog` `#E8EDF1` = **3.29:1**. The 11px `.hero__eyebrow`, `.section__eyebrow`, `.origin-card__region`, and `.footer__heading` fail.  
   - **Fix:** For small uppercase labels, drop the copper fill and use `--fog` on dark or `--ink-water` on light, letting copper appear only as underline/accent.

3. **Color contrast — CTA button text**  
   - **Rule:** WCAG AA normal text needs 4.5:1.  
   - **Fail:** `--pale-steel` `#F5F7F9` on `--copper-oxide` `#B86F4A` = **3.61:1**. The 14px button text is normal text, not large text.  
   - **Fix:** Darken the button background to ~`#8F4D32`, or use dark ink text on a lighter terracotta fill.

4. **Touch target size — footer links**  
   - **Rule:** Interactive touch targets must be at least 44×44 px.  
   - **Fail:** Footer links are inline with only `margin-bottom: 8px`; rendered height is roughly 14 px.  
   - **Fix:** Add `padding-block: 12px` to `.footer__links a` and keep adequate spacing.

5. **Readable font size on mobile**  
   - **Rule:** Body ~16 px floor; never ship sub-legible text to fit a layout.  
   - **Fail:** Multiple labels at `11px` (eyebrows, origin regions, footer headings) and `.footer__bottom` at `0.8rem` (~13 px) are hard to read on small viewports.  
   - **Fix:** Bump eyebrows to at least `12px` (prefer `13px`) and footer bottom to `0.9rem` or `1rem`.

**What passes**
- Viewport meta tag is present.
- Layout reflows without horizontal scroll at the breakpoints provided.
- `prefers-reduced-motion` is honored.
- SVG images and the tasting wheel have `aria-label` / `role="img"` or `aria-hidden="true"`.
- Focus rings are declared for the CTA and footer links.
