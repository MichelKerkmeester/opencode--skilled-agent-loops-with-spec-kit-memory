Common questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade, you get access to the new features immediately. If you downgrade, your storage limit applies on your next billing date, but nothing gets deleted.

Do I own my RSS feed?

Completely. Every plan includes a standard RSS 2.0 feed that points to your own domain if you want. You can export it and move to any other host at any time. We never hold your feed hostage.

What happens if I exceed my storage?

We notify you when you reach 80% of your storage limit. If you go over, your existing episodes stay live and downloadable, but you cannot upload new ones until you free space or upgrade.

Is there a free trial?

The Hobby plan has a free tier with 2 hours of storage and all the core features. Pro and Studio come with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required.
