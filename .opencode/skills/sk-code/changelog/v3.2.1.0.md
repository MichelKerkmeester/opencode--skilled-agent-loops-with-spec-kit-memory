## [**3.2.1.0**] - 2026-05-05

This release declares the cross-skill authoring-time load contract from sk-code's side. Pairs with system-spec-kit/SKILL.md §16-17 update and `cross_skill_authoring_load` block in `/speckit:complete:auto` and `:confirm` YAMLs (packet 078/002). When `/speckit:complete` writes inside `.opencode/`, sk-code now surfaces the relevant authoring checklist plus the spec_folder_write recipe BEFORE writes, not just at review-time. Closes 077 findings F-009-001, F-009-002, F-008-004, F-006-004.

> Spec folder: `specs/skilled-agent-orchestration/078-opencode-authoring-recipe/002-spec-kit-load` (Level 1, phase child)

## What Changed

#### Architecture
- **Cross-Skill Consumption block declared** -- sk-code/SKILL.md now lists which authoring checklist + recipe surface for each .opencode/ target type. Pull-side of the contract documented in system-spec-kit/SKILL.md.
- **Authoring-time vs review-time load split** -- /speckit:complete loads sk-code at TWO points: Step 10 (authoring) for .opencode/ targets, and Step 11 (review) as before. Authoring-time surfaces invariants the writer needs to honor; review-time catches drift.
