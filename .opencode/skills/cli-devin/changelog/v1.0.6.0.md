## Summary

Packet 118 ran the 5 council-seeded variants × 7 fixtures × 2 frontier models = 70 dispatches. Both SWE-1.6 findings that v1.0.5.0 had captured held cross-model with deltas above noise threshold:

| Gate | SWE-1.6 baseline Δ | deepseek-v4-pro Δ | kimi-k2.6 Δ | Verdict |
|------|---------------------|-------------------|-------------|---------|
| Bundle-gate-aversion (v-004 RCAF-medium > v-005 BUILD-strict) | +0.005 | +0.0525 | +0.0305 | HOLDS cross-model |
| Framework > anti-hallucination (v-004 > v-003 STAR+anti-halluc) | +0.050 | +0.0732 | +0.0756 | HOLDS cross-model |

**Winner per model**: `v-004-rcaf-medium` on **all three** measured models (SWE-1.6, deepseek-v4-pro, kimi-k2.6). The RCAF default established in v1.0.5.0 is now validated cross-model.

The packet 118 run also surfaced model-preset reliability quirks worth documenting in the skill itself:

- `--model deepseek-v4` is reasoning-bound and frequently exceeds 15 minutes on non-trivial fixtures (one packet 118 dispatch hit a 15-minute timeout under the harness's initial timeout setting). Recommend ≥ 25-minute per-dispatch timeouts for any long-running harness using this preset.
- `--model kimi-k2.6` occasionally hangs ~25 minutes on complex fixtures (~5–10% failure rate at default timeouts; packet 118 saw 2 such hangs out of 21 kimi dispatches). Bump timeout to 30+ minutes or aggregate over ≥ 5 fixtures.
- `--model swe-1.6` is the fastest preset and default timeouts (10–15 min) typically suffice.
