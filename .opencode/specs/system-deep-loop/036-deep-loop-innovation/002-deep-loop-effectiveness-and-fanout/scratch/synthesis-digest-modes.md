### Insights
- A research plan should be a versioned executable object: question dependencies, required evidence classes, counter-searches, and approval state must survive execution. _(ev: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/deep-research, high)_
- Research telemetry becomes actionable when costs are attributed to queries, citations, and supported claims rather than only to iterations or tokens. _(ev: https://docs.perplexity.ai/docs/sonar/models/sonar-deep-research, high)_
- The continuation primitive should be structured as gap plus originating evidence plus follow-up query plus expected closure condition. _(ev: https://github.com/langchain-ai/local-deep-researcher, high)_
- Maintaining and auditing a live draft during retrieval converts synthesis defects into targeted research actions and reduces information loss between investigation and final writing. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.16075, high)_
- The defensible state unit is not an iteration summary but a versioned claim-evidence decision: query recipe, source version, selector, support relation, inclusion decision, and supersession history. _(ev: https://www.prisma-statement.org/prisma-2020 and https://github.com/in-toto/attestation/blob/main/spec/v1/statement.md, high)_
- A living-research resume should rerun frozen search recipes, diff result IDs and content digests, screen only deltas, and propagate source changes through claim dependencies. _(ev: https://www.cochrane.org/authors/handbooks-and-manuals/handbook/current/chapter-22, high)_
- Question selection can be treated as active acquisition: learn expected evidence yield from prior branch outcomes and combine it with contradiction risk, source-independence gain, staleness, impact, and cost. _(ev: https://github.com/asreview/asreview, med)_
- Current deep-research benchmarks primarily grade terminal report quality; replay equivalence, updateability, evidence lineage, and change detection remain largely unmeasured and therefore form a credible differentiation axis. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11685 and https://github.com/imlrz/DeepResearch-Bench-II, high)_
- Quality guards that run only on STOP are too late: an invalid iteration can already poison strategy, findings, and progressive synthesis. Add a pre-reducer admission verdict that can preserve raw evidence while withholding it from trusted state. _(ev: https://github.com/amazon-science/RefChecker, high)_
- Citation reliability has two independent layers: source identity and claim entailment. A real paper with mismatched metadata fails identity; a correctly identified paper that does not support the claim fails entailment. _(ev: https://github.com/markrussinovich/refchecker ; https://github.com/amazon-science/RefChecker, high)_
- Raw novelty is not a safe convergence variable because fabrication, duplicated syndication, and poisoned content can all appear novel. Only admitted, independently sourced claim deltas should contribute to evidence yield. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01948 ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.04499, high)_
- Confirmation bias needs a claim-level falsification receipt, not generic source diversity. DeepTRACE found one-sided outputs even when deep-research configurations achieved high citation thoroughness. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.04499, high)_
- Reproducible failure evaluation needs controlled evidence, distractors, noise, and poisoned variants. Static sandboxes make false admission, missed evidence, and guard false positives measurable across runtime versions. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14683 ; https://github.com/microsoft/BIPIA, high)_
- The synthesis artifact should be a materialized view of a versioned atomic-claim ledger, with evidence-span and derivation edges, rather than the primary mutable state. _(ev: https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-o/ and https://github.com/trungdong/prov, high)_
- Support retrieval, contradiction retrieval, and answer generation should be separate operations so a fluent synthesis cannot conceal missing or conflicting evidence. _(ev: https://github.com/Future-House/paper-qa, high)_
- Claim evaluation should allow multiple valid evidence passages and score grounded abstention, not require one canonical citation or reward unsupported completeness. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11074, med)_
- Retraction and update metadata are useful change signals but unsafe as automatic truth; they should reopen dependent claims for verification. _(ev: https://github.com/ourresearch/openalex-docs/blob/main/api-entities/works/filter-works.md and https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.13339, high)_

### Contradictions / tensions
- **GPT Researcher argues that scraping more sites and selecting frequent information reduces incorrect or biased conclusions.** vs This is insufficient when sites copy a common upstream source; the transferable unit is independent claim support, while conflicting or inconclusive evidence must remain explicit. _(ev: https://github.com/assafelovic/gpt-researcher and https://github.com/GAIR-NLP/DeepResearcher)_
- **External JSONL state and deterministic resume make a research run replayable.** vs They make the control plane replayable only; web results, page contents, prompts, models, and stochastic reasoning must be versioned or frozen before evidence acquisition and conclusions can be reconstructed. _(ev: https://github.com/in-toto/attestation/blob/main/spec/v1/statement.md)_
- **High citation-quality scores demonstrate that a deep-research system is auditable.** vs Current benchmarks score the final report's citations, not search completeness, source-version integrity, inclusion decisions, replay equivalence, or correct propagation of later evidence changes. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11685; https://github.com/imlrz/DeepResearch-Bench-II)_
- **More citations and broader source diversity imply trustworthy research.** vs Citation-rich systems can remain one-sided, and many statements may still be unsupported by the cited sources; provenance clusters and entailment must be checked separately. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.04499)_
- **An automated claim checker can safely decide truth and convergence.** vs Claim extraction and NLI or LLM checking introduce their own errors. Uncertain cases should abstain or remain quarantined, with raw spans and verifier versions retained for replay. _(ev: https://github.com/amazon-science/RefChecker)_
- **OpenAlex is_retracted can serve as automatic claim-invalidation ground truth.** vs A published comparison reports retraction misclassifications; treat the field as a revalidation trigger and require confirmation from retraction notices, Crossref relations, or publishers. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.13339)_

### Insights
- A review pass should emit candidates, not verdict-bearing findings; semantic clustering, claim-level replication, and a separately prompted validator should precede P0/P1 activation. _(ev: https://cursor.com/blog/building-bugbot, high)_
- Impact, confidence, actionability, evidence type, and reachability are orthogonal. Encoding all of them in P0/P1/P2 makes low-confidence catastrophic hypotheses indistinguishable from reproduced blockers. _(ev: https://docs.devin.ai/work-with-devin/devin-review ; https://semgrep.dev/docs/semgrep-secrets/view-triage, high)_
- False-positive learning should produce scoped, inspectable suppression records with rationale, applicability conditions, expiry or invalidation triggers, and shadow-audit results—not opaque prompt memory. _(ev: https://semgrep.dev/blog/2025/making-zero-false-positive-sast-a-reality-with-ai-powered-memory/ ; https://www.greptile.com/docs/code-review-bot/custom-context, high)_
- The meaningful optimization target is a precision-recall frontier plus downstream resolution and rebuttal rates, stratified by category and severity; novelty convergence alone cannot detect a confidently noisy reviewer. _(ev: https://github.com/withmartian/code-review-benchmark ; https://www.qodo.ai/blog/how-we-built-a-real-world-benchmark-for-ai-code-review/, high)_
- Multi-review diversity should be treated as a recall stage, not a truth vote: aggregate candidates first, then require independent validation before publishing them. _(ev: SWR-Bench reports up to 43.67% F1 improvement from multi-review aggregation on 1,000 verified PRs: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.01494, high)_
- The useful unit of adversarial review is a falsifiable execution or data-flow claim, with intermediate facts retained so a validator can attack the weakest link. _(ev: PurCL/RepoAudit PathValidator and IntraDataFlowAnalyzer: https://github.com/PurCL/RepoAudit/wiki/02.-Project-Architecture, high)_
- Iterative verification should narrow around disputed findings and uncovered dimensions; whole-project repetition has weaker and less consistent returns. _(ev: Detect--Repair--Verify found bounded iteration better than single-pass but with clearer gains at narrower scopes: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23633, high)_
- Changed-line mutation testing can measure whether tests are sensitive to the reviewed behavior without paying whole-repository mutation cost. _(ev: Google incremental code-review mutation study and Stryker incremental line scoping: https://research.google/pubs/practical-mutation-testing-at-scale-a-view-from-google/ ; https://stryker-mutator.io/docs/stryker-js/incremental/, high)_
- The judge should see only the semantic residual left after schema, build, test, diff-filtered static analysis, and claim-specific executable checks; deterministic failures and eliminations remain first-class receipts. _(ev: https://github.com/reviewdog/reviewdog/blob/master/filter/filter.go ; https://semgrep.dev/docs/workflows/overview ; https://www.promptfoo.dev/docs/configuration/expected-outputs/, high)_
- Refute-by-N must count independent evidence classes rather than repeated LLM votes; require a two-sided executable control so a malformed generated reproducer cannot confirm its own finding. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19400, high)_
- Cross-pass finding identity should use versioned partial fingerprints based on symbols, normalized source context, program slices, and rename mapping; baseline state should distinguish new, updated, unchanged, and absent. _(ev: https://docs.oasis-open.org/sarif/sarif/v2.1.0/os/sarif-v2.1.0-os.html ; https://github.com/github/codeql-action/blob/main/src/fingerprints.ts, high)_
- P0/P1/P2 should remain an impact axis while confidence, reachability, exploitability, evidence strength, and evidence scope are recorded independently; otherwise repeated judge agreement can incorrectly inflate severity. _(ev: https://github.com/microsoft/sarif-sdk/blob/main/docs/ai/generating-sarif.md, high)_
- A blocking finding needs two independent axes: impact and evidential confidence. High impact alone is a verification priority, not proof. _(ev: https://github.com/crytic/slither/blob/master/slither/detectors/abstract_detector.py, high)_
- The proposed fix is a falsifiable counterfactual: execute original and revised implementations against existing and spec-augmented tests, and classify non-improving fixes as overcorrection evidence. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.00539, high)_
- Reviewer versions need shadow probation on known-clean and known-defective controls; pass health should gate whether any newly generated finding is admitted. _(ev: https://doc.rust-lang.org/stable/clippy/development/basics.html; https://docs.sonarsource.com/sonarqube-server/2026.1/user-guide/issues/solution-overview, high)_
- Coverage should be measured across reasoning distance, while context is escalated per cell. Flat full-context prompts can reduce detection even when token growth is small. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26130, med)_
- Sycophancy is detectable as evidence-invariant verdict instability: paired neutral and author-opinion renderings should produce identical severity unless new evidence is introduced. _(ev: https://github.com/kaustpradalab/LLM-sycophancy, med)_
- Construct a semantic residual by subtracting syntax-equivalent and explicitly modeled semantics-preserving edits before allocating LLM review passes. _(ev: https://github.com/facebook/infer/blob/main/Changelog.md and https://github.com/Wilfred/difftastic, high)_
- The natural path-coverage cell for deep-review is changed semantic node to affected observable boundary, represented as a program slice with explicit unmodeled external calls. _(ev: https://docs.joern.io/cpg-slicing/ and https://docs.joern.io/dataflow-semantics/, high)_
- Finding continuity should use introduced/fixed/preexisting lineage plus stable semantic anchors, allowing deep-review to distinguish a resolved defect from one moved or re-expressed by a later patch. _(ev: https://fbinfer.com/docs/steps-for-ci/, high)_
- A runtime difference is a witness, not a verdict: it must pass stability, causal-proximity, and relevance checks before changing finding confidence. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.11077, high)_
- Selective semantic-equivalence checking is best used as the expensive terminal verifier for scoped high-risk functions; inability to prove equivalence must remain distinct from a demonstrated behavior change. _(ev: https://github.com/diffkemp/diffkemp, high)_

### Contradictions / tensions
- **High comment precision or developer acceptance is sufficient evidence that a reviewer is effective.** vs High precision can hide very low recall, while developer action may reflect preference or workflow pressure rather than correctness. Pair fixed human-curated ground truth with fresh online outcomes and report both operating points. _(ev: https://www.qodo.ai/blog/how-we-built-a-real-world-benchmark-for-ai-code-review/ ; https://github.com/withmartian/code-review-benchmark)_
- **More review passes inherently improve review quality.** vs Bounded iteration improved secure-and-correct yield, but gains were uneven at project scope and clearer at narrower scopes; iteration needs adaptive per-finding targeting. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23633)_
- **A second LLM verdict constitutes independent verification.** vs RepoAudit combines parser-backed ordering checks with LLM path-contradiction analysis; the latter can still share correlated model errors, so executable or formal validators should outrank same-family re-judging. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.18160)_
- **File path plus absolute line range is a suitable primary cross-pass finding identity.** vs SARIF explicitly warns that absolute line numbers destabilize fingerprints after unrelated insertions; location should be supplemented by stable semantic and contextual components. _(ev: https://docs.oasis-open.org/sarif/sarif/v2.1.0/os/sarif-v2.1.0-os.html)_
- **Fresh-context repetition by the same reviewer is sufficient adversarial verification.** vs Fresh context reduces anchoring but does not create tool or evidence independence; CASCADE's false-positive control comes from complementary executions with opposite expected outcomes. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19400)_
- **Incremental mutation history can be reused whenever the directly mutated file is unchanged.** vs PIT warns that dependency changes can alter behavior without changing the mutated class and that several history optimizations introduce possible error. _(ev: https://pitest.org/quickstart/incremental_analysis/)_
- **More context and more requested reasoning should monotonically improve review accuracy.** vs SWE-PRBench reports all eight models degrading from diff-only toward fuller context, while the overcorrection study reports more explanations and proposed corrections increasing misjudgment of correct code. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26130; https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.00539)_
- **A loop that resolves or reports more issues is necessarily a better reviewer.** vs CR-Bench finds a resolution-versus-spurious-findings frontier and low signal-to-noise when agents are optimized to identify every hidden issue. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11078)_
- **A syntax-aware diff identifies behavior-preserving changes.** vs Difftastic explicitly compares syntax rather than semantics, treats ordering as significant, and falls back to line diffs on parse failures; AST equality is only a cheap filter. _(ev: https://github.com/Wilfred/difftastic)_
- **A correct runtime difference is relevant evidence for the reviewed change.** vs Collector-Sahab reported 96% correctness on 29 sampled augmented diffs but only 55% causal relation; timestamps, random paths, and unrelated state can dominate. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.11077)_
- **Failure to establish semantic equivalence demonstrates a behavioral regression.** vs DiffKemp is explicitly incomplete and can report false non-equivalence, especially under complex refactoring; unknown and counterexample must be separate outcomes. _(ev: https://github.com/diffkemp/diffkemp)_

### Insights
- Compile each council into shared common ground plus disjoint private evidence; exchange rationale excerpts so private signals can cross seat boundaries without collapsing the information partition. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/html/2607.01661, high)_
- Generate task-specific independent reasoning paths before fan-out, use unique paths for exploration and duplicate paths only when consistency checking is the better objective. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/html/2601.05746, high)_
- Construct recipient-specific inboxes from original dissenting message IDs; disagreement preservation is safer and more informative than confidence-threshold filtering or transcript summarization. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/html/2603.20640, high)_
- Track probabilistic beliefs, calibration, revisions, and oscillations as first-class council state; textual agreement alone cannot distinguish correct convergence from herding. _(ev: https://github.com/SpaceHunterInf/DMAD and https://github.com/tmlr-group/ECON, high)_
- Constitutional deliberation needs hard veto constraints, soft multi-objective scoring, and an explicit minority artifact because maximum consensus or cooperation can reduce autonomy, integrity, or fairness. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13189 and https://www.mac-prompt.com/, med)_
- Debate communication has no inherent positive drift: without a mechanism favoring independently supported correct signals, its expected gain can collapse to ordinary voting or stochastic re-evaluation. _(ev: https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper_files/paper/2025/file/934252acd87f254d5d4672fbde283bd2-Paper-Conference.pdf, high)_
- Effective seat count should be estimated from historical error correlation; architectural heterogeneity changes the scaling regime, while extra dense communication can destroy answer diversity. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02646, med)_
- Reasoning-method diversity is more load-bearing than persona diversity: seats need different algorithms or evidence partitions, not merely Analyst, Critic, and Pragmatist labels. _(ev: https://github.com/MraDonkey/DMAD, high)_
- Identity cues create measurable sycophancy and self-bias that accumulate across rounds; anonymized claim exchange and counterbalanced adjudication convert authority-sensitive debate into content-sensitive evaluation. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.07517, high)_
- Minority disagreement is an information channel, not convergence noise: debate dynamics can identify when a correlated majority suppressed the correct alternative, but an LLM judge may share the majority's blind spot. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29270, med)_
- Categorical council votes should be aggregated with task-conditioned seat confusion matrices and hierarchical priors; provider and prompt lineage must be modeled as correlated independence clusters or the posterior will overcount cloned errors. _(ev: pyro-ppl/pyro; https://github.com/pyro-ppl/pyro, high)_
- Blinded A/B and B/A judgments can be folded into a Bradley-Terry posterior with covariance; order disagreement is a judge defect or escalation signal, not an additional vote. _(ev: lucasmaystre/choix; https://choix.lum.li/en/latest/api.html, high)_
- Debate has its strongest empirical case under information asymmetry and unreliable consultancy; against direct answering without that asymmetry its benefit is mixed, so protocol routing should test for evidence asymmetry before paying for debate. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.08702 and https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.04622, high)_
- Seat dispatch can be formulated as Bayesian experimental design over seat, item, and protocol, optimizing expected entropy reduction per token instead of filling a fixed council roster. _(ev: pyro.contrib.oed; https://docs.pyro.ai/en/dev/contrib.oed.html, high)_
- Selective judge adaptation should trigger on swap inconsistency, but its evolving prompt is order-sensitive; calibration state must therefore be learned on a separate stream and frozen with a packet-visible version hash. _(ev: snumprlab/lwe; https://aclanthology.org/2026.eacl-short.50/, high)_
- A stance flip is evidence of learning only when it can be linked to newly introduced, independently verified claim IDs; majority-aligned flips without evidence gain are a direct conformity signal. _(ev: https://openreview.net/pdf?id=st77ShxP1K, high)_
- Falling disagreement is not inherently progress: track dispersion contraction jointly with verified-evidence gain and minority-claim disposition, because discussion can compress ratings and propagate biased opinions. _(ev: https://aclanthology.org/2024.emnlp-main.70/, high)_
- Majority size, perceived expertise, argument length, and rhetorical style are treatment variables; anonymization, length normalization, and order permutation can therefore serve as causal stability probes before convergence. _(ev: https://aclanthology.org/2026.acl-long.1756/, high)_
- Council synthesis should operate on atomic claims and exact leave-one-seat-out sensitivity, producing supported consensus, contested claims, and unresolved minorities rather than a scalar winner. _(ev: https://compdemocracy.org/polis-opinion-matrix/, med)_
- Council value is sharply task-conditional: hidden-profile and consequential process-accountability tasks are the plausible moat, while routine tasks should route to a strong single model. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11781, med)_
- Private pre-discussion estimates, evidence-focused discussion, and private post-discussion re-estimation separate information transfer from conformity and make influence measurable. _(ev: https://besjournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/2041-210X.12857, high)_
- A council verdict should preserve a pairwise option graph with ties and cycles; a Condorcet winner is useful when it exists, while a cycle is information about unresolved trade-offs rather than an aggregation failure. _(ev: https://github.com/voting-tools/pref_voting, high)_
- Counterfactual fork-and-diff turns susceptibility to speaker identity, order, verbosity, claimed expertise, and majority cues into a deterministic council quality test. _(ev: https://github.com/yoheinakajima/activegraph, high)_

### Contradictions / tensions
- **Using different model providers or more debate rounds inherently creates useful diversity.** vs Self-MoA can outperform heterogeneous MoA, mathematical MAD often gains little from agent diversity, and homogeneous evidence still induces correlated errors. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.00674, https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.22960, https://arxiv.org/html/2607.01661)_
- **More rounds monotonically improve the council by increasing convergence.** vs InfoDelphi's third round degraded both calibration and accuracy after private evidence had already propagated; later rounds shifted toward herding. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/html/2607.01661)_
- **Filtering to the most confident messages is the safest communication policy.** vs Confidence is often miscalibrated; DAR found that retaining authentic disagreement against the majority was more robust than threshold-based uncertainty filtering. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/html/2603.20640)_
- **Confidence-modulated debate can beat vanilla debate and majority voting when confidence is explicitly calibrated.** vs Raw self-reported confidence weighting produced no benefit for truthfulness, and controlled debate studies found confidence visibility secondary to reasoning strength and diversity; therefore confidence must remain hidden unless calibration is measured out of sample. _(ev: https://aclanthology.org/2026.findings-acl.1694/ ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06612 ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.07784)_
- **Additional debate rounds should improve plans through iterative correction.** vs Longer interaction can accumulate identity bias, reduce independent drafting diversity, and degrade performance; round continuation should require verified new evidence or resolved cruxes rather than movement toward agreement. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.07517 ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.19130 ; https://aclanthology.org/2026.findings-acl.1600/)_
- **Multi-agent debate is generally superior to a strong direct judge.** vs Debate reliably beat unreliable consultancy, but results against direct answering were mixed on tasks without information asymmetry. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.04622)_
- **A judge that learns during evaluation can safely improve the live council over time.** vs Selective LWE improves pair accuracy and consistency but is explicitly order-sensitive, so live adaptation can make verdicts depend on packet ordering unless calibration is separated and frozen. _(ev: https://aclanthology.org/2026.eacl-short.50/)_
- **Exposing calibrated peer confidence can make debate drift toward the correct hypothesis.** vs Perceived expertise, confident rhetoric, and longer arguments can independently increase conformity and reduce representative-agent accuracy. _(ev: https://aclanthology.org/2026.findings-acl.1694/ versus https://aclanthology.org/2026.acl-long.1756/)_
- **More structured multi-agent deliberation should generally outperform a strong single model.** vs The DCI study reports gains on non-routine and hidden-profile tasks, but the single-agent baseline wins overall and DCI consumes roughly 62 times the tokens. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11781)_

### Insights
- Treat evaluator traces, error messages, profiler output, and constraint failures as an actionable textual gradient; scalar dimension scores remain promotion constraints but are insufficient mutation guidance. _(ev: https://github.com/gepa-ai/gepa and https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.19457, high)_
- Maintain two selection structures: a case-wise Pareto frontier for complementary competence and a MAP-Elites archive over stable behavioral descriptors; do not use the five score dimensions themselves as permissive trade-off axes. _(ev: https://github.com/gepa-ai/gepa and https://github.com/algorithmicsuperintelligence/openevolve, high)_
- Mutation operators can themselves be candidates: record operator lineage and evolve diagnostic, crossover, and architecture-synthesis policies, but keep evaluator implementation and promotion thresholds outside the mutable genome. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.16797 and https://github.com/microsoft/stop, high)_
- A frozen LLM judge can still be gamed if it sees the optimized candidate before forming an independent answer; promotion should include a candidate-blind commitment or verifiable hidden anchor. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05904, med)_
- Under heterogeneous or drifting workloads, improvement should produce a routed tree of profile-specific champions; global replacement is reserved for candidates that transfer across profiles. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01770 and https://github.com/A-EVO-Lab/AdaptiveHarness, med)_
- A frozen evaluator is an experiment-scoped commitment, not a permanently trustworthy objective: re-baseline both candidate and baseline whenever an evaluator epoch changes. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.10760, high)_
- Evaluator secrecy and query control are separate from evaluator immutability; repeated scalar feedback can reveal exploitable judge preferences without any evaluator write access. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26156, high)_
- Require conservative cross-family judge agreement plus deterministic anchors; diverse reward-model ensembles using worst-case or uncertainty-weighted objectives reduce proxy overoptimization. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.02743, high)_
- The evaluator must be outside the mutable genome at the capability level, because models trained on specification gaming have generalized to modifying rewards and tests directly. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.10162, high)_
- Promotion is a deployment experiment rather than a file-copy decision: shadow evidence and bounded canary exposure measure behavior on the operating distribution while preserving a stable rollback target. _(ev: https://argo-rollouts.readthedocs.io/en/stable/features/analysis/, high)_
- Raw trial evidence must be append-only and reduction-independent: candidate, evaluator, fixture, seed, judge family, raw scale, rationale digest, normalization version, cost, and latency should survive every later score-policy change. _(ev: microsoft/waza internal/graders/grader.go and braintrustdata/autoevals, high)_
- Candidate-blind judging should run both presentation orders; disagreement between AB and BA is evidence of evaluator instability, not noise to average away silently. _(ev: https://github.com/confident-ai/deepeval/blob/main/deepeval/metrics/arena_g_eval/arena_g_eval.py, high)_
- Evolutionary mutation is useful for breadth, but survival should use per-dimension Pareto or lexicase selection so gains in one dimension cannot conceal regressions in another. _(ev: https://github.com/beeevita/EvoPrompt/blob/main/evoluter.py, high)_
- Metamorphic evaluation converts underspecified robustness expectations into executable paired oracles and can detect regressions without requiring a unique gold output. _(ev: https://github.com/steven-b-cho/llmorph and https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23611, high)_
- Classify every promotion failure as regressional, extremal, causal, or adversarial Goodhart and attach a distinct detection receipt and veto; generic benchmark_failed loses the evidence needed to improve the evaluator. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.04585, high)_
- Evaluator success and evaluator-integrity oversight must be separate outputs: a candidate can pass the nominal task while modifying reward, tests, caches, or evidence. _(ev: https://github.com/anthropics/sycophancy-to-subterfuge-paper, high)_
- Adaptive evaluator feedback is an information leak; reveal only thresholded verdict bands during optimization and withhold exact scores and fixture identities until session termination. _(ev: https://proceedings.mlr.press/v37/blum15.html, high)_
- Canary freshness needs an explicit lifecycle—sealed, active, burned, retired—and semantic leakage detection because paraphrases and translations bypass literal matching. _(ev: https://github.com/LiveBench/LiveBench; https://github.com/lm-sys/llm-decontaminator, high)_
- Optimization pressure is itself a risk signal: track cumulative evaluator queries and candidate fan-out against sealed/gold gain, and stop when proxy gain continues while independent gain stalls or reverses. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.10760, high)_
- Lane A currently generates separate candidate and baseline profiles, so the measured delta compares two subject-derived check sets; freezing weights alone does not freeze the evaluator. _(ev: Local source: scripts/agent-improvement/score-candidate.cjs lines 535 and 584; generate-profile.cjs buildProfile lines 263-282, high)_
- Evaluator identity should be a versioned dependency closure over the canonical profile, rubric, fixture commitments, grader, calibration, and environment; amendments open a new epoch rather than silently changing the ruler. _(ev: https://github.com/treeverse/dvc, high)_
- Metamorphic evaluation becomes substantially more actionable when relation families are generated and failing variants are shrunk into minimal counterexamples, rather than retained as an ad hoc list of perturbations. _(ev: https://github.com/HypothesisWorks/hypothesis ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23965, high)_
- Real-versus-test-cued twin fixtures detect deployment/evaluation divergence that ordinary held-out accuracy misses; this should be a hard parity gate for affected agent behaviors. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.14617 ; https://github.com/microsoft/Test_Awareness_Steering, high)_
- Prompt improvement needs representation regularization: recurrent, broadly supported rules should survive, while batch-specific patches and style-only rewrites should be filtered before expensive evaluation. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21318, high)_

### Contradictions / tensions
- **A frozen evaluator is sufficient to prevent self-improvement from gaming promotion.** vs A July 2026 preprint reports that even frozen reference-free judges optimize for candidate plausibility when shown the candidate first; blind commitment substantially reduced false positives. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05904)_
- **The best aggregate candidate should replace the current agent globally.** vs Adaptive Auto-Harness reports that one densely updated harness can peak and decline under heterogeneous, shifting streams; tree-based construction and solve-time routing preserve task-specific benefits. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.01770)_
- **The strongest available model should receive most of the improvement-generation budget.** vs Harness Updating Is Not Harness Benefit reports relatively flat harness-update gains across evolver capability tiers and non-monotonic ability of solver models to benefit from those updates. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30621)_
- **Freezing one evaluator is sufficient to prevent reward hacking.** vs Black-box bandit optimization can discover semantics-preserving score exploits, and preference attacks can transfer to judges not used during optimization; freezing must be combined with query control, evaluator diversity, adversarial canaries, and periodic human-authorized epoch rotation. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26156; https://papers.nips.cc/paper_files/paper/2025/hash/92a4d7a222ae16d4fbad90ef7743d5e0-Abstract-Conference.html)_
- **A rising aggregate evaluator score proves the candidate is improving.** vs Proxy reward can continue rising after gold reward peaks and declines; conservative ensemble objectives mitigate this better than single-proxy optimization. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.10760; https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.02743)_
- **A weighted aggregate plus fixed dimension floors is an adequate authoritative promotion score.** vs Evolutionary search optimizes whichever scalar is exposed, while heterogeneous dimensions have different noise and calibration; promotion should consume raw paired trials and dimension-level lower bounds, leaving the aggregate for ranking only. _(ev: https://github.com/beeevita/EvoPrompt/blob/main/evoluter.py and https://github.com/braintrustdata/autoevals)_
- **Rich evaluator rationales should be returned directly to the candidate generator because they accelerate improvement.** vs Raw rationales are valuable audit evidence but also expose an optimization oracle. Keep them inside the evaluator capsule and return only budgeted, redacted failure classes to mutation workers. _(ev: Inference from Autoevals raw metadata retention, DeepEval candidate masking, and Waza's narrow grader interface: https://github.com/braintrustdata/autoevals, https://github.com/confident-ai/deepeval, https://github.com/microsoft/waza)_
- **Increasing a frozen proxy score makes a candidate increasingly likely to be better.** vs Gold performance can peak and then decline as optimization pressure against an imperfect proxy increases; evaluator query count and candidate fan-out must be treated as risk variables. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.10760)_
- **A different-family LLM grader is sufficient evaluator independence.** vs Different-family grading removes one correlated-bias channel but does not make judgments objective or contamination-free; LiveBench adopted objective ground truth and reports severe judge error on hard tasks. _(ev: https://github.com/LiveBench/LiveBench/blob/main/changelog.md)_
- **Repeatability evidence is advisory only in the current promotion contract.** vs The shipped promotion path hard-fails when the repeatability report is missing or passed is not true; only weight recommendations are advisory. _(ev: .opencode/skills/system-deep-loop/deep-improvement/references/shared/promotion_gate_contract.md; .opencode/skills/system-deep-loop/deep-improvement/scripts/shared/promote-candidate.cjs)_
- **Dynamic five-dimension profiling provides a frozen evaluator and comparable candidate-minus-baseline deltas.** vs The scorer derives the candidate profile from the candidate and the baseline profile from the baseline, allowing the evaluated check set to change with the subject. _(ev: Local scripts/agent-improvement/score-candidate.cjs lines 535 and 584; generate-profile.cjs lines 263-282)_
- **Passing a static held-out aggregate is enough to establish deployment robustness.** vs Semantic-invariance and test-awareness studies show that equivalent phrasing or obvious test cues can materially change behavior while conventional benchmark performance remains superficially acceptable. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13173 ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.14617)_

### Insights
- Authority validity must be a prerequisite to artifact conformance: parse, type, capability, rule-test, coverage, and signature failures should produce AUTHORITY_INVALID rather than artifact PASS. _(ev: https://www.openpolicyagent.org/docs/cli and https://www.openpolicyagent.org/docs/management-bundles, high)_
- A typed rule IR should distinguish deterministic, schema, relational, and reasoning-required obligations so rule coverage cannot be confused with artifact coverage. _(ev: https://typespec.io/docs/extending-typespec/linters/, high)_
- Known deviations should be first-class, scope-predicated exception records whose use remains visible as SKIP or EXEMPT, not silent suppression. _(ev: https://kyverno.io/docs/guides/exceptions/ and https://kyverno.io/docs/guides/reports/, high)_
- Conformance is direction-sensitive across authority epochs: replaying witnesses from the old authority against the new one detects deleted or weakened obligations that a current-snapshot audit misses. _(ev: https://docs.specmatic.io/contract_driven_development/backward_compatibility.html, high)_
- Generated positive, negative, boundary, and stateful witnesses plus shrinking turn a semantic finding into a minimal replayable proof and reveal unexercised rule branches. _(ev: https://schemathesis.readthedocs.io/en/latest/explanations/data-generation/, high)_
- A proof-carrying finding should reference an immutable authority capsule, an explicit applicability decision, one or more subject-bound observations, and the evidence-backed verifier decision; otherwise it remains a candidate or inconclusive result. _(ev: https://pages.nist.gov/OSCAL/learn/concepts/layer/assessment/assessment-results/, high)_
- Applicability must precede verification. Lane workers should resolve target facts against authority-specific predicates and return not_applicable or unresolved before running expensive judges. _(ev: https://github.com/OpenSCAP/openscap/blob/main/docs/manual/manual.adoc, high)_
- Known deviations are safer as chronological assertions than filters: retain the original failure, record the accepted disposition, continue verification, and invalidate the assertion when authority, subject, verifier, scope, or time changes. _(ev: https://github.com/openvex/spec/blob/main/OPENVEX-SPEC.md, high)_
- False-positive control should measure effective usefulness, not technical correctness alone. Promote an authority/verifier pair only after shadow sampling shows its upper confidence bound meets the lane's usefulness threshold. _(ev: https://research.google.com/pubs/archive/43322.pdf, high)_
- Authority compilation should preserve source anchors, identifiers, profiles, applicability, and executable checks as separable layers; mutation tests can then detect deleted, weakened, inverted, narrowed, or exception-wrapped normative claims. _(ev: https://github.com/ComplianceAsCode/content, high)_
- Authority compilation needs a governed intermediate representation: LLM extraction may propose rule trees, but deterministic validation, witnesses, source spans, and authorized publication must gate execution. _(ev: https://aclanthology.org/2026.acl-industry.114/ ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17334, med)_
- Residual executable expressions provide a concrete absence-proof mechanism: unresolved required observations can remain explicit and yield INCONCLUSIVE instead of false conformity. _(ev: https://github.com/cel-expr/cel-go/blob/master/cel/env.go, high)_
- Suppression is a new adjudication fact, not a destructive rewrite of the original finding; authority or verifier changes can then reactivate it deterministically. _(ev: https://oss-review-toolkit.org/ort/docs/configuration/resolutions, high)_
- Cross-epoch conformance requires compatibility classes rather than one global pass/fail bit because the same change may preserve one contract while breaking another. _(ev: https://buf.build/docs/breaking/rules/, high)_
- Evidence receipts should be content-bound and freshness-aware so resume can distinguish verified observations from stale, missing, or unverifiable claims. _(ev: https://docs.sigstore.dev/cosign/verifying/verify/, high)_
- A readable live authority file is not necessarily current or authorized; conformance requires rollback, expiry, and mix-and-match checks over a pinned authority epoch. _(ev: https://github.com/theupdateframework/specification/blob/master/tuf-spec.md, high)_
- Suppression state can silently preserve obsolete verifier capabilities, so every deviation must bind to both an authority epoch and verifier digest. _(ev: https://github.com/Yelp/detect-secrets, high)_
- Completeness must be measured against declared applicability edges, not only discovered artifacts; otherwise a broken discovery adapter reports perfect coverage. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.13855, high)_
- Precision and recall can coexist through asymmetric verdict authority: high-precision rules may block, while extended rules create candidates requiring independent confirmation. _(ev: https://docs.github.com/en/code-security/concepts/code-scanning/codeql/codeql-query-suites, high)_
- An alignment certificate needs separate fields for requirement, claim, counterclaim, supporting evidence, counter-evidence, assessor, conformance, and confidence; combining these into one finding confidence loses the distinction between truth, evidence strength, and evaluator certainty. _(ev: https://github.com/CycloneDX/specification/blob/master/schema/bom-1.7.schema.json, high)_
- Replay must address subjects and verifier components by digest. Re-evaluating a mutable path or tag can validate a different object than the original audit and creates a time-of-check/time-of-use gap. _(ev: https://github.com/slsa-framework/slsa-verifier, high)_
- Structured assurance permits incremental semantic regression: propagate typed changes forward through evidence and backward through claims, preserving unaffected conclusions and marking only affected conclusions for replay or review. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.03026, high)_
- Alignment outcomes must distinguish failure from cannot-determine, inapplicability, and untested scope, and must record whether the judgment was automatic, semi-automatic, or manual; otherwise unknown coverage silently becomes PASS. _(ev: https://www.w3.org/TR/EARL10-Schema/, high)_

### Contradictions / tensions
- **Always reading the live authority gives the strongest verify-first result.** vs Without an immutable authority revision and digest, the same audit cannot be reproduced and an authority can change mid-run; snapshot the live authority at INIT and compare it with head at REPORT. _(ev: https://www.openpolicyagent.org/docs/management-bundles)_
- **Known deviations should be suppressed so accepted conventions do not appear as findings.** vs Suppression should change the result to an observable SKIP or EXEMPT receipt tied to an exception, not erase the evaluation; otherwise stale exceptions and overbroad matches are invisible. _(ev: https://kyverno.io/docs/guides/exceptions/ and https://kyverno.io/docs/guides/reports/)_
- **Authority compilation can eliminate reasoning-agent judgment.** vs OPA, TypeSpec, and similar systems compile formal languages, not arbitrary normative prose. Ambiguous obligations must remain explicitly reasoning-required and undergo witness or amendment review rather than being guessed into deterministic rules. _(ev: https://www.openpolicyagent.org/docs/policy-language and https://github.com/microsoft/typespec)_
- **Known-deviation suppression reduces noise.** vs Permanent or pre-execution suppression can hide changed conditions and real regressions; InSpec permits optional expiry and can skip a waived control entirely with run=false. _(ev: https://docs.chef.io/inspec/5.23/configure/waivers/)_
- **The newest negative assertion can safely override earlier findings.** vs Chronology resolves evolution but not authority: deep-alignment must also validate issuer authorization, authority precedence, subject identity, and compatibility before accepting an override. _(ev: https://github.com/openvex/spec/blob/main/OPENVEX-SPEC.md)_
- **A known deviation should remove or suppress a finding before it reaches persistent state.** vs ORT applies resolutions only during reporting while retaining analyzer and scanner evidence; deep-alignment should likewise append adjudication without deleting the raw fact. _(ev: https://oss-review-toolkit.org/ort/docs/configuration/resolutions)_
- **Conformance results remain comparable whenever the artifact itself has not changed.** vs Buf exposes several distinct compatibility contracts, showing that an authority change can preserve one dimension while invalidating another; unchanged artifacts may therefore require reclassification or replay. _(ev: https://buf.build/docs/breaking/rules/)_
- **A parsed and type-checked executable rule is sufficient evidence that natural-language authority was compiled correctly.** vs CEL can establish executable validity, but the policy-compilation evidence requires grounding, conflict checks, witnesses, and human correction or approval to establish semantic fidelity. _(ev: https://cel.dev/overview/cel-overview ; https://aclanthology.org/2026.acl-industry.114/)_
- **Re-verifying every finding against live ground truth is sufficient for high precision.** vs The same re-probe reproduces stale authority, stale verifier, and shared-model errors; authority and verifier provenance must be independently validated. _(ev: https://github.com/theupdateframework/specification/blob/master/tuf-spec.md)_
- **Checking 100% of discovered artifacts establishes alignment coverage.** vs A faulty discovery adapter or omitted lane shrinks the denominator and can produce a perfect score with silent violations. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.13855)_
- **A cryptographically signed alignment certificate proves that the audited system is conformant or safe.** vs A signature proves origin and integrity of the statement. Authority correctness, scope closure, policy evaluation, evidence sufficiency, and exception validity remain separate obligations. _(ev: https://docs.github.com/en/enterprise-cloud@latest/actions/concepts/security/artifact-attestations)_

- Promote a conditional operational envelope rather than a universal prompt because prompt structure, decoding configuration, model version, and task distribution interact. _(ev: https://aclanthology.org/2026.findings-acl.821/ ; https://proceedings.mlr.press/v293/spiess25a.html, high)_

- Negative capability is a first-class agent behavior: deliberate impossibility and ambiguity cases must test whether the candidate refuses, defers, or clarifies instead of maximizing nominal completion. _(ev: CAR-bench/car-bench — https://github.com/CAR-bench/car-bench, high)_
- A reusable normalized trajectory separates execution evidence from evaluator versions, allowing cheap evaluator replay while preserving the requirement to freshly execute every changed candidate. _(ev: agentevals-dev/agentevals — https://github.com/agentevals-dev/agentevals, high)_
- Reliable promotion needs two statistical regimes: zero-tolerance repeated passes for critical invariants, and paired family-level confidence bounds for broad task suites. _(ev: claw-eval/claw-eval — https://github.com/claw-eval/claw-eval and CAR-bench/car-bench — https://github.com/CAR-bench/car-bench, high)_
- Structural error analysis plus automatic rollback and a guard-metric floor can make prompt optimization monotone on measured objectives, but agent definitions additionally require authority, side-effect, and inheritance invariants. _(ev: SPEAR — https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26275, med)_
- Known-defect injection at a declared prompt, tool, routing, memory, or executor locus makes causal-blame accuracy measurable instead of inferred from aggregate improvement. _(ev: https://github.com/agiresearch/ASB/tree/main/config, high)_
- The useful optimizer is nested: reflective failure analysis proposes atomic diffs, while frontier search preserves behaviorally distinct candidates until ablation establishes causality. _(ev: https://github.com/maitrix-org/PromptAgent/blob/main/src/prompt_optim_agent/search_algo/mcts.py and https://github.com/sentient-agi/EvoSkill/blob/main/src/loop/runner.py, high)_
- Executor portability is an experimental axis: identical task artifacts and verifier semantics must be replayed through versioned executor adapters before promotion. _(ev: https://github.com/harbor-framework/harbor/tree/main/src/harbor/agents/installed, high)_
- Verifier isolation belongs in the behavioral contract; changing the verifier, adapter, tool schema, or model invalidates prior promotion evidence. _(ev: https://github.com/harbor-framework/harbor/blob/main/src/harbor/trial/trial.py, high)_
- Agent robustness should remain a family-level Pareto vector with confidence intervals because defensive gains can conceal clean-task capability loss. _(ev: https://github.com/normster/SystemCheck/blob/main/evals/EVALS.md, med)_
- Identical-replay stability does not detect prompt brittleness; promotion needs semantic-equivalence sets and a worst-variant or lower-tail gate. _(ev: https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/hash/7fa5a377b7ffabcce43cd00231bb3f9c-Abstract-Conference.html, high)_
- Cross-specification and worst-family retention are necessary because aggregate task or rubric scores can conceal severe rare-family regressions. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.08425, high)_
- Agent optimization must hold out environments and interaction patterns, not merely examples; held-in success can reflect memorized observation-action relations rather than adaptation. _(ev: https://proceedings.iclr.cc/paper_files/paper/2025/hash/a3cc50126338b175e56bb3cad134db0b-Abstract-Conference.html, high)_
- Missing dimension samples currently masquerade as perfect stability, so the evidence gate can confuse absence of measurement with repeatability. _(ev: .opencode/skills/system-deep-loop/deep-improvement/scripts/agent-improvement/benchmark-stability.cjs, high)_
- An agent-definition diff should compile into clause-level positive obligations, preserved-behavior controls, authority-conflict tests, perturbation families, and trace assertions. _(ev: https://github.com/invariantlabs-ai/invariant ; https://github.com/langwatch/scenario, high)_
- Behavioral semantic versioning can be operational: patch means equivalence across all families, minor means localized gain plus non-inferiority elsewhere, and major means authority or capability change requiring full requalification. _(ev: Synthesis from executable trace policies and scenario-based regression testing: https://github.com/invariantlabs-ai/invariant, med)_
- Adaptive item selection should optimize information about the paired candidate-baseline delta, subject to minimum family coverage and exposure caps, rather than optimize absolute candidate ability. _(ev: https://github.com/Peiyu-Georgia-Li/ATLAS ; https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.08240, high)_
- Failure localization should use clause/context and executor/policy interventions with repeated forward replay; visual trace inspection or judge attribution alone is not causal evidence. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08275 ; https://github.com/jaineet17/causal-agent-replay, med)_

### Contradictions / tensions
- **Self-evolving agent systems autonomously produce better agents.** vs Most demonstrate improvement on optimizer-visible proxy tasks; SePO has not released code, while SwarmAgentic explicitly warns that generated code may be destructive. Autonomous score improvement is not a safety or deployment guarantee. _(ev: https://github.com/taowangcheng/SePO ; https://github.com/yaoz720/SwarmAgenticCode#safety-considerations)_
- **The highest aggregate-scoring candidate is the best agent definition.** vs GEPA retains candidates that dominate individual cases or objectives, and AutoPDL finds that optimal prompting strategies vary across models and tasks. Promotion should preserve Pareto alternatives or conditional configurations. _(ev: https://github.com/gepa-ai/gepa ; https://proceedings.mlr.press/v293/spiess25a.html)_
- **Textual gradients provide causal credit assignment.** vs They are LLM-generated diagnoses, not causal proof. The proposed mutation still requires matched counterfactual replay, component ablation, and sealed evaluation before promotion. _(ev: https://github.com/aiwaves-cn/agents/blob/master/src/agents/optimization/node_optimizer.py ; https://github.com/AgentOpt/OpenTrace)_
- **Pass^3 should be the universal promotion metric.** vs It is appropriate for critical invariants, but applying it to every broad or expensive scenario is cost-heavy and brittle; paired confidence bounds are better for ordinary families. _(ev: https://github.com/claw-eval/claw-eval)_
- **Stored trace replay can replace candidate re-execution.** vs Replay isolates evaluator changes but cannot reveal how a changed agent definition would alter tool selection or control flow; candidates still require fresh paired executions. _(ev: https://github.com/agentevals-dev/agentevals)_
- **Executable validators remove the need for semantic judges.** vs They are superior for constraints and side effects, but user satisfaction and semantically equivalent plans still require calibrated semantic evaluation. _(ev: https://github.com/Junjie-Ye/CCTU; https://github.com/CAR-bench/car-bench)_
- **Scalar validation improvement is sufficient evidence that an agent-package mutation is better.** vs Broad mutations can trade away clean utility, improve only one executor, or repair the wrong locus; promotion needs family vectors, ablation, and transfer evidence. _(ev: https://github.com/sentient-agi/EvoSkill/blob/main/docs/architecture.md and https://github.com/maitrix-org/PromptAgent/blob/main/src/prompt_optim_agent/world_model/world_model.py)_
- **ASB's perturbation results can be reused directly as regression-grade agent-improvement evidence.** vs Its locus taxonomy is valuable, but parts of the runner use substring success checks and an LLM refusal judge; promotion requires deterministic receipts and an isolated verifier. _(ev: https://github.com/agiresearch/ASB/blob/main/main_attacker.py)_
- **Low variance across repeated benchmark runs demonstrates a robust prompt.** vs A prompt can be perfectly repeatable yet fail badly under semantically equivalent wording; RobustAlpacaEval observed a 45.48-point best-to-worst spread in one model. _(ev: https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/hash/7fa5a377b7ffabcce43cd00231bb3f9c-Abstract-Conference.html)_
- **A held-out aggregate score prevents benchmark overfitting.** vs Held-out data can still encode a narrow proxy, and aggregate performance can hide severe degradation in rare behaviors or other specifications. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.08425)_
- **Improvement on several known agent environments demonstrates general agent capability.** vs AgentRefine reports held-in success alongside failure on unseen environments caused by memorized observation-action relations. _(ev: https://proceedings.iclr.cc/paper_files/paper/2025/hash/a3cc50126338b175e56bb3cad134db0b-Abstract-Conference.html)_
- **An aggregate held-out score improvement is sufficient evidence to promote an agent prompt.** vs Agent definitions contain multiple preserved behavioral families; aggregate lift can hide regressions, and adaptive-evaluation research shows equal accuracy can conceal materially different ability profiles. _(ev: https://pypi.org/project/promptim/ ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.04689)_
- **A judge or human reading the failed trace can reliably identify the responsible step.** vs Trace attribution is correlational; counterfactual intervention is needed to distinguish the visible harmful action from the earlier decision that committed the agent to it. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08275)_

### Insights
- Router-A versus live-B is a model comparison only if model and execution path are independently crossed; otherwise the valid estimand is the difference between two complete candidate stacks. _(ev: https://inspect.aisi.org.uk/reference/inspect_ai.html and https://github.com/lmarena/p2l, high)_
- Adaptive evaluation should select cases by expected information gain after minimum family coverage, using a Rasch-style ability/difficulty estimate rather than spending the same number of trials on obvious wins and ambiguous boundaries. _(ev: https://crfm.stanford.edu/2025/06/04/reliable-and-efficient-evaluation.html, high)_
- Judge calibration must be candidate-specific: use a small oracle slice to correct paired judge deltas and propagate calibration uncertainty; shared calibration can produce a confident sign reversal. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06939 and https://www.amazon.science/publications/precise-reducing-the-bias-of-llm-evaluations-using-prediction-powered-ranking-estimation, high)_
- Contamination resistance is a case-lineage state machine: temporal source evidence, proposer visibility, oracle visibility, first exposure, disclosure, retirement, and replacement lineage must be recorded separately. _(ev: https://github.com/LiveBench/LiveBench, https://github.com/forecastingresearch/forecastbench, and https://github.com/Code2Bench/Code2Bench, high)_
- Public rankings should be treated as candidate-selection priors, because their top model and ordering often fail to transfer to unseen target tasks; report private-task rank correlation and public-top-1 regret explicitly. _(ev: https://openreview.net/forum?id=kVrKQ83PPo, high)_
- The outcome metric is part of the model-selection treatment: compliance checks, numeric equivalence, executable assertions, and task-specific ground truth can reorder models relative to generic lexical or aggregate metrics. _(ev: https://aclanthology.org/2025.chomps-main.1/, med)_
- Protocol robustness must be crossed with model and task rather than averaged away; benign formatting and system-prompt variations can move rankings or overturn deployment priorities. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.01781 and https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.02515, high)_
- Contamination resistance needs provenance, time separation, secrecy, rotation, and retirement lineage; literal duplicate detection alone cannot detect training on the broader test task. _(ev: https://proceedings.iclr.cc/paper_files/paper/2025/hash/ab8c971c2ccd12bac0ab249f75e2c16d-Abstract-Conference.html and https://github.com/LiveCodeBench/LiveCodeBench, high)_
- Cost-aware selection should optimize a quality-constrained Pareto frontier using complete realized cost and latency, then allocate further samples by expected decision value rather than applying a fixed weighted score. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08665 and https://github.com/vllm-project/guidellm, med)_
- A benchmark needs two item pools: common sealed anchors for paired promotion inference and an adaptive diagnostic tail selected by item information under explicit family-coverage quotas. _(ev: https://github.com/allenai/fluid-benchmarking, high)_
- The correct resampling unit is the task or item family, with seeds and perturbations nested beneath it; flattening fixture-sample rows understates uncertainty when variants share semantics. _(ev: https://github.com/ianarawjo/evalstats, high)_
- Judge outputs should be treated as noisy predictions corrected by sparse randomized oracle labels per dimension and task cluster, not as ground truth validated only by global agreement. _(ev: https://github.com/ianarawjo/evalstats, high)_
- Contamination is a versioned item-level evidence record containing detector, matched span or hash, source scope, visibility, and lineage; exact n-grams are a cheap first detector but cannot establish paraphrase cleanliness. _(ev: https://github.com/huggingface/datatrove/blob/main/src/datatrove/pipeline/decont/n_grams.py, high)_
- Pairwise rank and operational value are different estimands: fit Bradley-Terry or Davidson for preference probabilities, then apply hard quality floors and uncertain business utility to choose a deployment policy. _(ev: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9374650/, high)_
- The shipped two-grader mechanism is not on the scoring path, and its two calls share one model family; reviewer independence must be represented by model-build provenance and enforced by dispatch, not inferred from different role prompts. _(ev: https://github.com/Shreyaskc/BabelJudge, high)_
- A multi-axis rubric is valid only if isolated axis perturbations move the intended subscore, leave unrelated subscores stable, and the reducer-owned total explains the final verdict; factor collapse or low schematic adherence makes the aggregate non-actionable. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.20293, high)_
- Contamination should be measured as non-generalizing performance inflation relative to a fresh same-task suite and a reference-model difficulty curve, with promotion based on whether the inflation interval or exclusions change the local winner. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.16281, high)_
- Sample adequacy is a property of the intended claim and independent item families, not a universal repetitions-per-cell constant; three samples can establish neither useful power nor task-distribution coverage. _(ev: https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/bowyer25a.html, high)_
- Gold truth needs component-level provenance, independent reviews, final adjudication, an explicit uncertain state, and selective replay when an oracle changes; model consensus is diagnostic evidence rather than authority. _(ev: https://github.com/SKYLENAGE-AI/HLE-Verified, high)_
- The strongest mode-specific experimental unit is a frozen workflow prefix plus environment snapshot: replay the same state while intervening only on model identity, then compare resulting actions and state transitions. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07184, high)_
- A benchmark winner is not decision-grade unless the paired design has enough resolution for the observed gap; repeated decoding must be clustered by underlying task and comparisons across candidates corrected for multiplicity. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30315, high)_
- Cost-aware selection should price task errors, latency, abstention, and switching overhead in the user's operational units; token price alone can favor a cheap model whose mistakes dominate total economic loss. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.03834, high)_
- Benchmark identity must include both the semantic evaluation recipe and the deployment workload profile; identical quality fixtures can produce different valid selections under different context-length, concurrency, and tail-latency regimes. _(ev: https://github.com/IBM/unitxt and https://github.com/llm-d/llm-d-benchmark/tree/main/workload, high)_

### Contradictions / tensions
- **A single aggregate five-dimensional ranking is sufficient for model selection.** vs Prompt-to-Leaderboard finds prompt-dependent Bradley-Terry strengths specifically because marginal rankings conceal prompt-level performance reversals. _(ev: https://github.com/lmarena/p2l)_
- **One judge calibration can be reused across compared candidate models.** vs Cross-model calibration instability can bias the comparison enough to reverse its sign with high apparent confidence. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06939)_
- **A recent timestamp alone makes a benchmark contamination-free.** vs Temporal filtering lowers risk but does not prove non-exposure; sealed future-resolved questions or private generated cases with explicit exposure lineage provide stronger evidence. _(ev: https://github.com/forecastingresearch/forecastbench and https://github.com/Code2Bench/Code2Bench)_
- **A generic public-leaderboard winner is a reliable default model for an unseen workflow.** vs Leaderboard-to-target correlations were below 0.5 for most tested tasks, and the leaderboard winner was best on only one target task. _(ev: https://openreview.net/forum?id=kVrKQ83PPo)_
- **The current model-benchmark lane already performs cost-aware selection.** vs dispatch-model.cjs exposes normalized latency, token, and cost fields, but sweep-benchmark.cjs hardcodes token and cost fields to null and sweep-reporter.cjs ranks efficiency by output word count. _(ev: .opencode/skills/system-deep-loop/deep-improvement/scripts/model-benchmark/dispatch-model.cjs:295; sweep-benchmark.cjs:467; lib/sweep-reporter.cjs:71)_
- **Router replay is model-benchmark Mode A and live execution is its Mode B in the current checkout.** vs model-benchmark/MODES.md labels model-vs-model as A and framework bakeoff as B; router-replay versus live execution is documented under skill-benchmark, creating a naming collision. _(ev: .opencode/skills/system-deep-loop/deep-improvement/scripts/model-benchmark/MODES.md:18; scripts/skill-benchmark/tests/README.md:33)_
- **Adaptive testing always improves benchmark efficiency without changing the claim.** vs Candidate-specific item paths destroy direct paired comparability and may omit rare failure families; adaptive trials need common anchors, coverage quotas, and either confirmatory separation or sequentially valid inference. _(ev: https://github.com/allenai/fluid-benchmarking)_
- **Position swapping should be applied to every LLM-judge comparison.** vs The April 2026 study found position bias small for its tested judges while style bias dominated, making universal swapping a potentially expensive treatment for the wrong measured bias. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23178)_
- **Quality divided by dollar is a sufficient cost-normalized model score.** vs Ratios are unstable near zero, hide hard quality regressions, and cannot express latency or asymmetric failure costs; quality floors plus Pareto and net-utility sensitivity analysis are safer. _(ev: https://github.com/sksoumik/llm-as-judge)_
- **A primary and skeptic call constitute two independent reviewers.** vs Both current calls use the same Claude-family grader and shared evidence; different prompts can reveal stance sensitivity but cannot cancel shared self-preference or family bias, and the helper is not wired into the scorer. _(ev: https://github.com/Shreyaskc/BabelJudge)_
- **Controlled degradation creates free gold labels.** vs The label is valid only when the reference is correct and the transformation monotonically worsens the target construct without altering others; otherwise the generated audit probe inherits a gold error. _(ev: https://github.com/Shreyaskc/BabelJudge)_
- **A repeatable benchmark-pass is sufficient promotion evidence.** vs A contaminated fixture, biased judge, collapsed rubric, or wrong oracle can repeat perfectly; repeatability is one validity component, not validity itself. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.20293)_
- **A verified benchmark snapshot can be referenced by name alone.** vs The HLE-Verified arXiv abstract reports 641 verified and 1,170 revised items, while the current repository reports 668 Gold and 1,143 Revision items; snapshot hashes and version-bound counts are necessary. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.13964)_
- **The highest weighted five-dimension score identifies the best model.** vs Small score gaps may be statistically unresolved, and plausible changes to utility weights or task clustering can reverse the winner; the correct result may be a Pareto set, conditional route, or insufficient evidence. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30315 and https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.03834)_
- **A curated task suite is sufficient to predict workflow behavior after switching models.** vs Curated evaluations can be distributionally unrepresentative and recognizable as tests; replaying representative deployment prefixes improved real-deployment forecasts, although environment and tool fidelity remain limiting factors. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07184)_

### Insights
- Skill lift must be a within-task, within-executor paired contrast; an absolute with-skill score mostly measures the underlying executor and task difficulty. _(ev: https://www.skillsbench.ai/blogs/skillsbench-1-1, high)_
- Availability, invocation, trajectory compliance, and outcome are separate causal stages; primary ranking should use intention-to-treat lift while the later stages diagnose why lift was gained or lost. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20087, high)_
- Near-neighbor and noise skills are necessary negative controls because a preselected-skill benchmark does not measure discovery, routing, or library interference. _(ev: https://github.com/langchain-ai/skills-benchmarks, high)_
- A skill score without environment and dependency compatibility metadata is unsafe: version-mismatched procedural guidance can measurably reduce success. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15401, high)_
- Gold scoring should combine deterministic final-state checks, non-binding milestone-DAG diagnostics, and executable dynamic reference functions rather than require one canonical trajectory or static answer. _(ev: https://github.com/apple/ToolSandbox ; https://github.com/SalesforceAIResearch/MCP-Universe, high)_
- Skill value is a staged treatment: discovery, loading, instruction adherence, and verified outcome must be measured separately because oracle-injected gains collapse under realistic retrieval. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.04323, high)_
- A balanced off/auto/forced/placebo design identifies realized lift, selection tax, and content effect while executor-by-skill interactions distinguish model dependence from loader dependence. _(ev: https://github.com/py-why/EconML, high)_
- Unique canaries embedded separately in SKILL.md, references, scripts, and assets produce objective exposure receipts and avoid relying on model self-report. _(ev: https://github.com/agent-ecosystem/agent-skill-implementation/blob/main/benchmark-skills/README.md, high)_
- Task success is insufficient test adequacy: only 38.66-45.51% of extracted skill behavior constraints were covered in evaluated trajectories, so skill-benchmark needs constraint coverage alongside outcome lift. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20659, high)_
- The correct product of skill-benchmark is a versioned effect certificate with a validity domain, not a leaderboard row. _(ev: https://github.com/NVIDIA/skills/blob/main/skills/skill-card-generator/BENCHMARK.md, high)_
- Three randomized arms separate deployable intention-to-treat lift from forced-use capability and expose a diagnostic routing gap; observational activation traces alone cannot make that decomposition causal. _(ev: https://github.com/NVIDIA/skills/blob/main/skills/skill-card-generator/skill-card.md, high)_
- Skill quality is ecology-dependent: discovery degrades with registry complexity, while safety failures can emerge only along composed activation paths. _(ev: https://github.com/ynulihao/AgentSkillOS; https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15242, high)_
- A two-stage design—fractional-factorial screening followed by exact confirmation of selected interactions—can attribute bundle effects without evaluating the full component power set. _(ev: https://salib.readthedocs.io/en/latest/api/SALib.analyze.html; https://github.com/shap/shap, high)_

### Contradictions / tensions
- **Broad curated-skill evaluation reports a +16.6 percentage-point aggregate lift.** vs Requirement-driven SWE evaluation reports only +1.2 points on average, with 39 of 49 skills showing zero gain and three showing negative lift; task selection, baseline saturation, specialization, and compatibility therefore dominate any universal skill-effect claim. _(ev: https://www.skillsbench.ai/blogs/skillsbench-1-1 ; https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15401)_
- **A standardized Agent Skills folder is portable, so one executor is sufficient for skill evaluation.** vs The standard defines package shape and progressive disclosure, while real harnesses may strip metadata, preload or hide resources, prune instructions, or use different activation mechanisms. _(ev: https://github.com/agent-ecosystem/agent-skill-implementation)_
- **A Mode A PASS establishes that the skill is effective.** vs Current Mode A normalizes over router dimensions while task usefulness remains unscored; realistic retrieval experiments show that oracle-route utility can fall toward the no-skill baseline. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.04323)_
- **A signed or verified skill is an effective skill.** vs A signature establishes artifact integrity, not causal efficacy; effectiveness requires separate counterfactual evidence and a validity domain. _(ev: https://github.com/NVIDIA/skills#verifying-skills)_
- **A skill's isolated benchmark score is sufficient for deployment.** vs Composition can create capability-flow, trust-transfer, and authorization-confusion failures that are absent in isolated arms. _(ev: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15242)_
- **One skill-on versus skill-off score explains why value was gained or lost.** vs That comparison estimates an aggregate treatment effect but cannot distinguish discovery, activation, guidance, execution, or component interactions without additional randomized arms. _(ev: https://github.com/NVIDIA/skills/blob/main/skills/skill-card-generator/BENCHMARK.md)_
