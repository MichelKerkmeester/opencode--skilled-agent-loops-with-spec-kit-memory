## 2. TOPIC
Study Hallmark as a design skill for AI coding assistants and produce a licensed, evidence-grounded reuse and learning matrix for surgical improvements to the shipped sk-design hub, its five mode packets, commands, audit checklists, design-reference/MD-generator pipeline, and styles corpus.
