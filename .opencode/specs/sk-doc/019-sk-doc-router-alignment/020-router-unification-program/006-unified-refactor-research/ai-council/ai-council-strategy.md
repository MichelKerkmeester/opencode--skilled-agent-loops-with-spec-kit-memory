- **Seat 001 — Minimalist / degenerate-case purist** (Pragmatic/Minimalist lens). Owns the graceful-degradation test: the N=1 case must be the SAME contract with zero special-casing and near-zero overhead. Resists field over-emission.
- **Seat 002 — Safety / authority hardliner** (Critical lens). Owns destination-local authority, the "negatives withhold authority / carry no target" invariant, proof-is-evidence, the never-touch-the-scorer and no-over-emission constraints, and the hard activation gates.
- **Seat 003 — Migration / adaptivity realist** (Holistic lens, carrying the learning/adaptivity view). Owns the reversible gated migration, real-hub falsification, the `(T,R,P)` posture, and the offline correction overlay (kept off-by-default, offline-gated).

Note on seat count: the agent contract caps a council at 3 seats. The operator listed four candidate lenses; the learning/adaptivity lens is carried inside Seat 003 and stress-tested by Seats 001 and 002 rather than given a fourth seat, to respect the cap while surfacing the genuine minimalism-vs-adaptivity disagreement.

## Convergence rule
`two-of-three-agree`: 2 of 3 seats endorsing essentially the same fused design with no new high-severity finding from cross-critique declares convergence. Genuine convergence is expected here because four independent research lineages already converged; the council's job is to detect artificial vs. genuine convergence and surface real residual disagreements.

## Known constraints (non-negotiable, carried into every seat)
- Deterministic offline route-gold replay preserved; NEVER edit the shared scorer (`router-replay.cjs`).
- Authority stays destination-local; a proof/recommendation is never a capability.
- Reversible + gated migration; no over-emission (never union the full registry into scored routes).
- Graceful degradation to singular skills is a requirement, not a nice-to-have.
