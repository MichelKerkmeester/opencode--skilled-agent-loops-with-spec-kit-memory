- Is the ~335 LOC claim realistic? NO — summed individual estimates total ~550 LOC. Realistic scope for the listed changes is ~430 LOC (±50). Re-baseline needed.

- Does deterministic rendering have hidden complexity? YES — named-principle classification (design_md_format.md:544) requires AI judgment about token patterns that cannot be fully automated. Citation gating proves token grounding, not claim truth. The [INFERRED] label system is honest about this limitation.
