# v1.0.0.0 - Data-backed and Claude Design parity

**Released:** 2026-06-14

This initial release ships `interface` as a data-backed judgment skill that also shares a Claude Design parity loop with `mcp-magicpath`, while keeping it lean and anti-default. It vendors Anthropic's frontend-design skill - a SKILL.md plus one design-principles reference, all guidance and no data - and builds on that baseline with design data, an optional search, additional references, and the shared parity protocol. The design process and the anti-default mandate come from the upstream skill and are preserved unchanged.

Driven by the research-and-build phases under spec `143-interface` (002 merge research, 003 merge, 004 sk-doc alignment, 005-007 Claude Design parity, 008 doc realignment).

---

## What's Included

### Design data and search (the ui-ux-pro-max merge)

- `assets/data/` - nine MIT-licensed CSVs adopted from the `ui-ux-pro-max` repo: the quality-floor sets (`ux-guidelines`, `charts`, `app-interface`) and the aesthetic inventory (`styles`, `colors`, `typography`, `ui-reasoning`, `products`, `landing`).
- `scripts/design_search{,_core}.py` - an optional, zero-dependency BM25 search over the data, query-only (the upstream design-system generator and persistence were deliberately not adopted).
- The aesthetic data is framed strictly as a critique-against inventory, never a chooser, to protect the anti-default philosophy.

### References

- `references/design-process/design-principles.md` - the vendored authority for palette, type, structure, motion, and copy (kept byte-for-byte except house-voice fixes).
- `references/design-process/ux-quality-reference.md` - the objective quality floor (accessibility, motion, touch, responsive, forms, charts) used as the pass/fail gate.
- `references/design-grounding/design-inventory.md` - the aesthetic data as common patterns to deviate from.
- `references/claude_design_parity.md` - the shared cross-skill parity protocol: ground in a design system, reuse before generating, an element-target revision grammar, a `previewImageUrl` fidelity check gated on the quality floor plus the anti-default critique, a generated/presentational boundary, an optional handoff block, and a hard no-style-presets guardrail.

### sk-doc alignment

- `feature-catalog/` - the skill's features catalogued, including the Claude Design parity loop section (13 feature entries).
- `manual-testing-playbook/` - manual scenarios, including reuse-before-generate and the previewImageUrl fidelity check with negative controls (9 scenarios).
- All references aligned to the sk-doc reference template.

### Licensing

- `LICENSE-ui-ux-pro-max.txt` (verbatim MIT) + `THIRD-PARTY-NOTICES.md` - the skill is mixed-license: Apache-2.0 (principles) plus MIT (data and search), notice retained.

### Routing

- `graph-metadata.json` - trigger phrases (color palette, font pairing, design tokens, ux checklist) and key_files for the data, search, and parity surfaces; the `enhances sk-code` and `prerequisite_for mcp-magicpath` edges set.
