Yirgacheffe

Worka Sakaro Washing Station, Gedeo Zone

Altitude

Process

Jasmine and bergamot on the nose. Stone fruit sweetness with a clean, tea-like body. We've bought from Worka Sakaro since 2021 — their washed lots are the most consistent in the Gedeo region.

Huehuetenango

Finca El Injerto, La Libertad

Altitude
1,800–1,950m
Process
Honey
Variety
Bourbon / Caturra
Harvest
Jan 2025

Brown sugar and dried apricot with a rounded, syrupy finish. The honey process gives this lot more body than our typical single-origins — excellent as a morning filter brew.
