This release makes a small, safe follow-up condensation of `SKILL.md`: the Codex Agent Delegation table drops its duplicated per-row invocation-example column and points to `references/agent_delegation.md` (which already holds the full roster and invocation patterns). No routing behavior, rule, model, or contract changed.

`SKILL.md` was already condensed once (v1.6.1.0, 4630 to 3976 words), so the remaining content is largely load-bearing. The verbose ALWAYS rules 10 to 16 were deliberately preserved verbatim rather than tightened -- they are functional dispatch contracts where a small word saving is not worth any risk of dropping a directive.
