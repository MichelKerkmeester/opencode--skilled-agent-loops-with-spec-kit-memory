# Hallmark adoption for sk-design

## 1. METADATA

- **Track:** sk-design
- **Packet:** 016-hallmark-adoption (phase parent)
- **Source research:** `sk-design/014-hallmark-design-skill-research` (20-iteration deep research; two independent GPT-5.6-SOL lineages; no early convergence)
- **License basis:** Hallmark is MIT-licensed — clean-room adaptation preferred; substantial copies carry the MIT notice; external assets are out of scope.

## 2. PROBLEM & PURPOSE

The 014 study concluded: Hallmark is a source of **surgical heuristics and vocabulary**, not a replacement — sk-design's five modes already own its four verbs. This packet folds the highest-value ideas into the **existing** modes via clean-room adaptation across four gated phases: surgical fixes, shared evidence envelopes, authored decision cards, and one genuinely new brand-first capability. **No new modes or commands**; the measured-vs-authored boundary is an absolute invariant.

## 3. SCOPE

- **In:** the phased adoption plan and its gates, decomposed into the four phase children below.
- **Out:** building anything in this parent; new modes/commands; copying Hallmark's themes, macrostructure/component catalogs, or third-party assets.
