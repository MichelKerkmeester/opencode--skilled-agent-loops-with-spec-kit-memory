> Spec folder: `.opencode/specs/skilled-agent-orchestration/124-sk-prompt-parent` (8-phase program)

## Why

`sk-prompt` and `sk-prompt-models` were two flat, independently advisor-routable skills with no structural relationship to each other â€” no shared registry, no advisor graph edge, tagged only by a common `family: sk-util`. Each README narrated the boundary against the other in prose, but the advisor saw two unrelated identities. This repo's parent-hub canon (already applied to `sk-code`, `sk-design`, `system-deep-loop`, and `sk-doc`) exists to fix exactly this shape.

## What Changed

- Two `packetKind: "workflow"` modes: `prompt-improve` (mutating, the 7-framework/DEPTH/CLEAR engine) and `prompt-models` (read-only, per-model prompt-craft profiles). - `graph-metadata.json` keeps the `sk-prompt` identity, folding in `sk-prompt-models`' `enhances â†’ cli-opencode` edge (raised to weight `0.8`, the stronger empirical relationship) and its domain/intent-signal content.
