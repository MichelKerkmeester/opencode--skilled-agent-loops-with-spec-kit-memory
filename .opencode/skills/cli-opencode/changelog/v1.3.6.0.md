## Research Findings (126/003)

- **Model**: Xiaomi MiMo-V2.5-Pro (released 2026-04-22). MoE ~1.02T total / 42B active; multimodal.
- **Context window**: **1,000,000 tokens** (native 32K → RoPE-extended to 1M; usable long-context retention past 512k).
- **Endpoint**: OpenAI-compatible `https://token-plan-ams.xiaomimimo.com/v1` (`@ai-sdk/openai-compatible`); opencode cost shows `0/0` → **Token-Plan subscription** billing.
- **Agentic**: sustains 1000+ sequential tool calls; SWE-bench Pro 57.2; **40–60% fewer tokens/trajectory** than frontier models.
- **`--variant`**: maps to MiMo's reasoning effort (low/medium/high) in opencode settings; **always dispatch with `--variant high`** (confirmed accepted on opencode 1.15.13; operator-directed standing default).

## Benchmark Findings (126/004)

- **Winner: COSTAR + lean-to-medium pre-planning** (composite 1.0000). **RACE** is a statistical-tie fallback (0.9934; margin within single-sample noise).
- **MiMo is the opposite of MiniMax.** The guardrail-heavy TIDD-EC that won for MiniMax (120/003) ranked **DEAD LAST** for MiMo.
- All 5 frameworks scored **100% correctness** (MiMo is frontier-class), so the discriminator is **format adherence + token efficiency, not correctness.** Lean output/audience-framed prompts suppress the explanatory preamble that CIDI/RCAF/TIDD-EC leak on harder tasks (TIDD-EC ~2.4× longer output).
- **Avoid CIDI** — its process framing intermittently makes MiMo write to a file via a tool call instead of returning inline code (~22% of observed dispatches).
- One-liner: **MiMo is frontier-correct already — frame for format + brevity, not for guardrails.**

## Caveats

- Benchmark: single sample per (framework × fixture), 2 deterministic fixtures, fully deterministic scoring (no LLM-judge). COSTAR vs RACE is a tie within noise. Rig left ready-to-run: `node eval/run-mimo-bench.cjs --model xiaomi-token-plan-ams/mimo-v2.5-pro --timeout 120`.
- The framework benchmark was **re-run at `--variant high`** for both MiMo and MiniMax (a 2×2: model × default/high). **COSTAR holds as co-winner for MiMo at high reasoning** — at high the field *compresses* (even TIDD-EC's verbosity penalty largely vanishes: 151→66 words on the hard fixture; format-adherent frameworks went 2/5→4/5), so framework choice matters *less* at high reasoning, but COSTAR/RACE-lean stays the safe default. MiniMax → RACE on this 2-fixture rig — do NOT overwrite the 7-fixture 120/003 TIDD-EC recommendation (correctness saturates on 2 tractable fixtures). Framework (COSTAR) and reasoning (`--variant high`) are independent, composable levers.
- Long-context quality degrades past ~512k despite the 1M window; Token-Plan request-window semantics remain unverified.
