# Soft Preset

A compact visual-direction cue for premium agency-style interfaces that need haptic depth, generous rhythm, and fluid but disciplined motion.

---

## 1. OVERVIEW

### Purpose

Use for premium agency-style interfaces that need haptic depth, generous rhythm, and fluid but disciplined motion.

### Usage

Apply this preset as a citeable starting cue, then adapt it to the product context instead of treating it as a fixed template. Use one preset as the dominant aesthetic per direction; if combining cues, name this as the primary preset and keep borrowed cues sparse.

---

## 2. PALETTE

- Choose one texture family: OLED black with muted radial glows, warm cream with sage or espresso, or silver-white structural surfaces.
- Keep color atmospheric instead of loud: deep blacks, warm creams, muted sage, soft metal grays, and low-opacity accent light.
- Avoid generic bright SaaS primaries and harsh dark shadows.

---

## 3. TYPE

- Use premium-feeling display and UI faces such as Geist, Clash Display, PP Editorial New, or Plus Jakarta Sans.
- Combine massive grotesque headings with fine editorial contrast where the brand supports it.
- Eyebrow labels are tiny, uppercase, widely tracked pills.

---

## 4. SHAPE AND LAYOUT

- Select one dominant layout archetype: asymmetrical bento, z-axis card cascade, or editorial split.
- Major surfaces use nested enclosures: an outer shell with hairline ring and padding, then an inner core with its own surface and highlight.
- Use large radii around `2rem`, but collapse rotations, overlaps, and asymmetric spans on mobile.

---

## 5. MOTION AND TEXTURE

- Use custom spring-like easing, never default linear or ease-in-out.
- Scroll reveals can fade up from `translateY(16px)` with slight blur resolving over `800ms+`.
- Animate only transform and opacity; keep backdrop blur on fixed or sticky layers, not scrolling content.
