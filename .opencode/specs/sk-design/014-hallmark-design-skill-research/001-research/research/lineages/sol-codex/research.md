# Hallmark Design Skill → sk-design Reuse and Capability Research

## 1. Executive Summary

Hallmark is useful as a source of surgical heuristics, not as a replacement for the shipped sk-design hub. The highest-value adoptions are seven missing anti-slop probes, a conditional measured Motion section in the v3 `DESIGN.md` pipeline, an authored multi-page coherence card, measured composition-DNA fields and retrieval facets, semantic motion-character handoff, a provider-neutral owned-asset manifest, and a small independently authored structural-fingerprint card set. A brand-first authored-system lane is the only genuinely new user-facing capability worth designing; redesign, variants, data visualization, study, explanation, and render critique already have owners.

## 2. Copy-vs-Learn Licensing Verdict

Hallmark is MIT-licensed. Use, copying, modification, publication, distribution, sublicensing, and sale are permitted, but copies or substantial portions must retain the copyright, permission notice, and disclaimer. Close prose, tables, code, or catalog reuse therefore needs a third-party notice surface carrying the complete Hallmark notice. Independent concepts and newly authored implementations are `ADAPT` or `LEARN`; close unattributed paraphrase is not. The repository license does not establish redistribution rights for externally hosted images, attributed quotations, logos, or other third-party material, so those remain `SKIP` unless asset-specific provenance is confirmed. ## 3. Scope and Method

Ten forced-depth iterations read Hallmark's license, `SKILL.md`, README, ROADMAP, all 24 top-level references, the four verb contracts, 21 macrostructure packets, 50 component packets, canonical 58-gate checklist, theme system, and roadmap proposals. They compared those sources against sk-design's hub, five mode packets, `/interface:*` commands, audit/fingerprint machinery, MD-generator v3 schema/backend, motion contracts, foundations references, interface procedures, styles database, and corpus retrieval. Convergence stayed telemetry-only until iteration 10; every iteration produced a cited narrative, canonical JSONL record, and delta artifact.
